
![[Pasted image 20250522224308.png]]

--- end-multi-column

Myogenic nature - Involuntary, generates own muscle activity

Modification via [[Processes affected by ANS action|ANS]]

- Filaments connected in mesh like network not in series
- No troponin + extra intermediate filament to support shape
- Ca2+ acts on thick filament instead

TLDR;  Ca2+ create enzyme to activate another enzyme to help ATP bind to myosin cross bridge head → can bind with actin
