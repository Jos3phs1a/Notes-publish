**Single contractile unit of muscles**
foundation of [[Type of muscle fibers|msucle fibers]]

![[Pasted image 20250522010534.png]]

Formed by branches of:

[[Molecular Anatomy#Actin|Actin]] molecule →  6 thin filament
[[Molecular Anatomy#Myosin|Myosin]] molecule → 1 thick filament

M Line: Mid line of sarcomere
Z line: border of sarcomere
## HAI

H zone: Only thick filament found
A band: Width of the thick filament
I band: Only thin filaments found

During contraction: actin filaments pulled in, entire sarcomere length decrease

H zone: shortens
A band: only increases in size relative to entire sarcomere
I band: thin filaments shorten, width shortens
