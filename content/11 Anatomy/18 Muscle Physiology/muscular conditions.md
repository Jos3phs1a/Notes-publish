## Atrophy
loss of muscle tissue and tone, causing ↓ muscle mass, strength, mobility

mainly due to lack of stimulation
- Disuse
- Denervation
- Aging
Disease e.g. Amyotrophic lateral sclerosis(ALS), multiple sclerosis (MS)

## Sarcopenia
Gradual loss of muscle size and muscle fibres during ageing process
Loss is accelerated as we age
Loss is permanent and may affect bone mass


## Postmortem(death) rigor(stiff) mortis
Ca$^{2+}$ not removed from [[Molecular Anatomy#Actin|actin]], muscles stay in contracted state
