Form of ==afferent input== in muscles

Function: communicate changes in length to [[01a Anatomical divisions#1. Central Nervous System(CNS)|CNS]] to maintain muscle proprioception during [[muscle contraction]]

Stimulus: mechanical stress

Mechanism: to maintain function, spindle must remain taut and stretch with [[Type of muscle fibers|muscle fibers]] accordingly, via alpha gamma coactivation
