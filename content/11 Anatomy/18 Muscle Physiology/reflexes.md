movements that are involuntary, sensory signals skip the [[01ba Anatomic Divisions of Brain|brain]] before movement signals are sent from the [[01c Spinal Cord|spinal cord]]
## Monosynaptic
Sensory neuron → motor neuron without relaying to [[01a Anatomical divisions#1. Central Nervous System(CNS)|CNS]] first
e.g. stretch reflex: knee, bicep jerk

## Polysynaptic

More than 1 synapse involved, interneurons involved to target multiple muscles

### Withdrawal reflex
2 muscle groups involved
- touching hot stove and hand pulls away, both ==biceps(agonistic)== and ==triceps(antagonistic)== activate

### Crossed Extensor reflex
2 limbs controlled
- Step on nail, affected leg lifts up, unaffected leg lands to maintain balance
