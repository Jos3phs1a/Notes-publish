## Class 1 MHC (Intracellular)
- Detected by [[White blood cells#^fc4a1b|CD8 cytotoxic T cells]]
- found in endoplasmic reticulum of all nucleated cells
- pick up antigens and bring to cell surface
## Class 2 MHC (Extracellular)
- Detected by [[White blood cells#^fc4a1b|CD4 Helper T cells]] 
- found in membranes of APCs e.g. dendritic cells, macrophages, and B cells
