# Immunodeficiency

- occur due to system failing to respond
- may be genetic or acquired, impair [[Antibody-mediated immunity]] and or [[cell-mediated immunity]]

# Autoimmune

- malfunction in immune system, ==causing it to attack host body== and body's cells
- Activated B cells make antibodies (**Autoantibodies/Auto-abs**) against body cells and tissues

#### Thyroiditis
release auto-abs against thyroglobulin, a precursor of thyroid hormones

#### Rheumatoid arthritis:
auto-abs form immune complex within connective tissue of joints
# Allergies

- inappropriate immune response to antigens
- caused by [[allergens]] 

4 types only tested on
#### Type 1
Immediate hypersensitivity
- occurs when response occurs within 30 minutes of exposure to allergen
- [[Antibody-mediated immunity#^568059|IgE]] production activating [[White blood cells#Basophils|Basophils & mast cells]] causing inflammation 
e.g. hay fever, pollens, pets 

can lead to swelling → [[anaphylaxis]]
#### Type 4
delayed hypersensitivity 
- mediated by T lymphocytes, 
- peak intensity 1-3 days after contact with allergen
- unknown antigens accidentally trigger langerhan cells to trigger immune response → [[Inflammation]] 

Treatment: steroid creams
