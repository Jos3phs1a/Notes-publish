## Classical pathway


--- start-multi-column: ID_kt8v
```column-settings
Number of Columns: 2
Largest Column: standard
```

1) antigens on pathogen recognised by antigen
2) ==antigen binds to antigen== forming **antigen-antibody complex**
3) ==C1 protein binds to complex== and gets activated
4) ==C2 & C4 split== into fragments and bind with each other → ==C3 Convertase== 
5) C3 Convertase splits into ==C3a(attracts phagocytes)== and ==C3b(mark for death)==
6) C3b also joins other C proteins (C5-8) to form **membrane attack complex(MAC)**
7) membrane attack complex ==opens hole in target cell== → water rush in, ==target cell rupture==
