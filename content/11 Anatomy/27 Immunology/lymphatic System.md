# Lymph
[[Blood Composition#Plasma|plasma]] of lymph vessels 
- lower concentration of proteins, nutrients, waste etc

# Lymph vessels
- Vessels that transport lymph 
- Walls formed by overlapping **endothelial cells** with ==gaps== that open due to osmotic pressure on walls
- similar to veins, rely on *smooth muscles* on inner lining of endothelial cells + *valves* 
# Lymphoid tissues
Tissues that produce, store and process [[White blood cells#Lymphocytes|Lymphocytes]]
located ==close to== but *==do not contain==* [[lymphatic System#Lymph nodes|Lymph nodes]] and ==substance entry point to body==

Include:
- bone marrow
- lymph nodes
- spleen
- thymus
- tonsils
- adenoids
- appendix 
- Peyer's patches
- gut associated lymphoid tissue

# Lymph nodes

Organ that purifies lymph before return to venous circulation
Removes:
- debris
- pathogen
- majority antigens

# Lymphatic Ducts

2 ducts, 
#### right lymphatic duct
Drain from right upper body + limb
right jugular trunk, right subclavian trunk, right bronchiomediastinal trunk 
empties into **right subclavian vein**
#### Thoracic duct 
Drain from rest of the body 

empties into **left subclavian vein**
