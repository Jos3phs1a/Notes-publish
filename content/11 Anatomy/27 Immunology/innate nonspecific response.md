## Types of resistance
1) Physical barriers
	block hazardous materials outside body
	Skin, transitional epithelium urinary bladder, gut epithelial cells
2) Phagocytic cells
	attack and remove microorganisms
3) [[Immunological surveillance]]
	constantly monitors normal tissues
4) [[Interferons]]
	anti-viral proteins block viral replication
5) [[The Complement system]]
	plasma proteins attack plasma membrane
