# Anatomy
2 components,
Levator ani
- Pubo-coccygeus
- Puborectalis
- Ilio-coccygeus

Coccygeus

| Muscles            | Origin                                                    | Insertion                                       | Innervation                                  |
| ------------------ | --------------------------------------------------------- | ----------------------------------------------- | -------------------------------------------- |
| **Pubo-coccygeus** | posterior surface of the pubis(lateral to puborectalis)   | Coccyx, Ano coccygeal body(Ane-coccygeal raphe) | perineal branches of Pudendal nerve (S2- S4) |
| **Puborectalis**   | Posterior surface of bodies of pubic bones                | None, forms puborectoral sling                  | ^                                            |
| **Ilio-coccygeus** | Tendinous arch of interal obturator fascia, Ischial spine | Anococcygeal ligament(raphe), Coccyx            | ^                                            |
| **Coccygeus**      | ischial spines                                            | inferior end of the sacrum and coccyx           | S4-5                                         |

# Function

- Support of pelvic viscera
- Defecation:
	- coccygeus pulls coccyx forwards after displaced backwards during defecation
	- Pubo-rectal sling relax to allow straight passage of rectum & anal canal
- Gender based:
	- Male: elevate prostate
	- constrict vagina-sphincter vaginae
