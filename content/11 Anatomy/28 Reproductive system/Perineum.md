# Subdivisions

2 subdivisions divided by imaginary line between left & right ischial tuberosities
## Urogenital Triangle
Borders from ==pubis symphysis== along inferior pubis to ==ischial tuberosities==

Contain the [[Genitalia]]

## Anal Triangle
Borders from tip of ==coccyx== to ischial tuberosities
