## Rhesus group
- Guiding Questions
	- What are the groups for?
	- What impact do they have on each other
Sub group presence of Rh factor protein denoted by '+' & '-'
 =='-' can donate to '+'== because '+' body will not see as threat
=='+' cannot donate to '-'== because '-' body will view threat because protein is considered foreign
