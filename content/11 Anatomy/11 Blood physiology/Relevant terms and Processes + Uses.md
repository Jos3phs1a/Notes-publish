### Prothrombin Time(PT)
- Time for plasma to clot after **F3** and **F**4** added(simulate tissue dmg, [[Hemostasis^252b81|extrinsic pathway]])
- **Normal range: 11-13s**

### INR number(international normalized ratio)
$$INR(\frac{PTtest}{PTnormal})^{ISI} $$

- Used to measure ==blood viscosity== ^4cf079
- Lower test time -> Blood thick clot fast  Higher test time -> Blood thin, clot slow
- ==Ideal range: 1.2-2.0, <1.2 = too thick, >2.0 = too thin==

### Activated Partial Thromboplastin Time(aPTT)

- Time for plasma to clot after F12+exposed collagen mimic e.g. silica added(simulate internal dmg, [[Hemostasis#^252b81|intrinsic pathway)]]
- **Normal range: 25-40s**

### [[Blood Conditions & Disorders|Clinical Uses]]:
- Info to administer appropriate medicine
- e.g. anticoagulant, coagulant medicines, platelet injections, vitamin K

| Anticoagulants                                  | Coagulants               |
| ----------------------------------------------- | ------------------------ |
| Heparin: inhibits conversion of F2(prothrombin) | Vitamin K (Phytonadione) |
| Anti Thrombin III                               | Fibrinogen Concentrate   |
| Warfarin drugs                                  | Platelet injections      |
