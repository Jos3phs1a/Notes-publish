## Process
- Guiding Questions
	- What are the crucial stages of Hemostasis?

1. **Vascular Spasm:**
	Vasoconstriction of Smooth muscle

2. [[Function of platelets in Hemostasis#Platelet Phase|Platelet Plug Formation]]
	Aggregation of platelets to form plug + release chemical substances for (3).

3. **Coagulation**
	Stoppage of bleeding via [[Hemostasis#^252b81|Extrinsic & Intrinsic pathways]] → lead to Common pathway → formation of fibrin polymer

## Regulating Factors
- Guiding Questions
	- What substance affect hemostasis?
	- What would happen without them?

**Vitamin K** - used to product factors **10,9,7,2**
	Found in meat, leafy greens, avocado
	Less vita K = clotting problems → excess bleeding ^f20729

**[[Blood Composition#Platelets|Platelet]] count** - low count → excessive bleeding as platelet plug forms too slow

**[[Relevant terms and Processes + Uses#^4cf079|Blood Viscosity]]:**
	too thin → excess bleeding
	too thick → coagulation factors too inefficient, risk of thrombus(vessel clot)
