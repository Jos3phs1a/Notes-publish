## [[Blood Composition]] related
### Anemia
Insufficient ability to transport O2

Microcytic
	 [[Erythropoiesis process and factors#^d6a8a6|iron]] deficiency -> ==RBCs smaller== than normal
Macrocytic
	lack of [[Erythropoiesis process and factors#^d6a8a6|B12 or folate]] -> ==RBCs bigger== than usual
Aplastic
	Radiation or chemo -> ==Failure of bone marrow== to make RBCs,
### Polycythemia 

Excess of RBCs
**Primary:**
	Tumor/ tumor-like condition affecting bone marrow
**Secondary:**
	EPO induced, to improve O2 transport e.g. high altitudes
**Relative:**
	RBC vol no change, but less Plasma vol, %vol RBC increase e.g. dehydration

## Clotting Related

### Thromboembolism
**Thrombus:**
	Clot in the bloodstream
	Block circulation → [[Heart Conditions & Disorders#^3417b6|Ischemia]]

**Embolus:**
	Thrombus floating in bloodstream

### Deficiency
Lack of these substances lead to clotting problems
**Thrombocytopenia:**
	Suppression or Destruction of bone marrow → [[Function of platelets in Hemostasis|Platelet]] count too low <50mil/ml
	Widespread hemorrhages → bruising on skin

**Hemophilia**
	Clotting problems
	A -> lack F8 (genetic)
	B -> lack F9 ([[Hemostasis#^f20729|vitamin K]] deficiency)
	C -> lack F11 (genetic)
