Produced by RBCs glycolysis as side product

binds to deoxyhemoglobin and ==facilitate oxygen transport==. 
When bound to deoxyhemoglobin, deoxyhemoglobin molecule is stabilized, and equilibrium between deoxyhemoglobin and oxyhemoglobin ==shifts toward deoxyhemoglobin==. 
This interaction shifts the [[oxygen dissociation curve]] to the right, ==decreasing the oxygen affinity== of hemoglobin and enhancing oxygen delivery to tissues.
