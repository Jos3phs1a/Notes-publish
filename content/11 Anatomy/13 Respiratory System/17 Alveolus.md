## function
facilitate gas exchange between inhaled air and blood


# Structure

facilitate [[#function]] via:
- abundance of capillaries
- small size + large surface area
### type 1 cells:
- simple [[Types of Epithelial Tissue|squamous epithelium]] for gas exchange
- occupies most of the surface in alveolus ~90%

### type 2 cells:
- surfactant (phospholipid) synthesis → prevent collapse of alveoli
- alveolar macrophages:
	digests debris in air


**sites of gas exchange:**
not smooth → large surface area in contact with capillaries

contact time between blood and capillary & alveolus ~ - 0.75s at rest, blood fully oxygenated at ~ 0.25s

facilitate [[Ventilation and Perfusion]]
