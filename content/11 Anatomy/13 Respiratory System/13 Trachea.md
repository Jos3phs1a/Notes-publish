# Structure

- starts from C6(cervical vertebrae) **divides** around T4 (thoracic vertebrae)
- **Carina** = point of division → become [[14 Bronchi & Bronchioles|bronchi]] 

- ==top half in neck==, rest of it in [[Anatomy of heart, great vessels, mediastinum structures#Location of the heart|Superior mediastinum]]
