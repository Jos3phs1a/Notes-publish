
### **Compliance:**

- allows chest walls and lungs to stretch
- measure of lung stretchability = Change in lung vol/Change in airway pressure
- decrease in compliance and stretchability means muscles work more
    - recall expiration is passive, ↓compliance → accessory muscles work

major determinant: surface tension of alveoli

caused by liquid film within microscopic alveoli

space between liquid v small → liquid molecules stick together → generate surface tension(T)

r = radius of alveoli

**P∝T** and **P∝1/r**

hence smaller ones tend to collapse pushing gas into larger alveoli

**to increase compliance: alveolar uses surfactant**

surfactant contains phospholipids, lipids, proteins

lines inner surface of alveolar epithelium
