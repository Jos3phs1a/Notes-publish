### **Thoracic cage:**
- 12 pairs of [[ribs]] 
- ribs 8-10 are false ribs:
    - do not connect directly to sternum, use the 7th’s costal cartilage
- last 2 Floating Ribs → not connected to the sternum at all

**costal margin:**
	joins the cartilages of ribs 7-10

### **sternum:**

- split into 3 segments
    - manubrium
    - body
    - xyphoid process

### Intercostal muscles

stiffen chest walls
active during breathing: assist in changing thoracic volume

**External intercostal: 1st layer**
	runs downwards and forwards, at adjacent borders of upper and lower ribs
function:
	inspiration → elevate ribs

**internal intercostal: 2nd layer**
	runs downwards and backwards at adjacent borders of upper and lower ribs
function:
	depress ribs in forced expiration

**Innermost Intercostal: 3rd layer**

**Transverse thoracis:**
	back on sternum to costal cartilages

**Innermost intercostal:**
	side of upper to lower ribs on inner surfaces

**Subcostalis:**
	posterior part of ribs on their inner surfaces

**Intercostal space:**
	cavity between internal and innermost layer, contains vessels and nerves in upper section of space
