## function
- protects [[15 lungs|lungs]] by acting as shock absorbers
- contributes to [[Mechanism of Ventilation]]
## Structure
### Parietal pleurae:
lines thoracic wall, reaches base of neck, reaches [[15 Lungs#^550db1|Hilum]] reflects into visceral pleurae

### Visceral pleurae
covers outer surface of [[15 Lungs|Lungs]], extending into fissures between lobes

### Pleurae cavity
space containing [[#Pleurae fluid]]

### Pleurae fluid
- moist, slippery coating, provides lubrication. 
- Allows tissues to glide smoothly during breathing
- Aids in pulling visceral and parietal pleura allowing thoracic cavity expansion 
- creates [[Mechanism of Ventilation#Negative intrapleural pressure|intrapleural pressure]] for ventilation
