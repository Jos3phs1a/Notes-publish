how body prevents food from entering respiratory tract

[[11 Pharynx]]
soft palate: prevents food from entering nose


[[12 Larynx]]
**epiglottis**: folds to push food towards esophagus, away from trachea

vocal folds adduct and close to block food
pulls up and becomes narrower, esophagus more space to receive food

**cessation of breathing**: when swallowing food, body stops taking in air to prevent food from entering trachea
