## Micro
trachea & bronchi: cartilage + little smooth muscle

--- start-multi-column: ID_zjzu
```column-settings
Number of Columns: 2
Largest Column: standard
```

### Terminal Bronchioles
formed as bronchi divides within lungs:
- no more cartilage present
- more smooth muscles
- sparse glands and goblet cells → replaced by **club cells** no columnar epithelium
### Respiratory Bronchioles
- walls thinner than [[#Terminal Bronchioles]], allowing gaseous exchange
- start of **respiratory zone**
