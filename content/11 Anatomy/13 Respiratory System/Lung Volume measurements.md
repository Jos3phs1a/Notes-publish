Passive inspiration/expiration + functional residual capacity affected by elastic recoil of lungs and chest walls
Not affected by strength of muscles

Active inspiration/expiration is affected by strength of respiratory muscles.

### Total lung capacity
Maximum air lungs can hold
TLC= VC+RV

### Vital Capacity
Maximum amount of moveable air in the lungs via max inhalation + max expiration ~4600ml

### Tidal Volume
Volume of air inhaled or exhaled in a normal breath ~500ml
TV = TLC - FRC - IRV


--- column-break ---

### Inspiratory capacity
Volume of air during MAX inspiration
IC = IRV + TV

### Inspiratory reserve volume
EXTRA vol of air during MAX inspiration AFTER normal inhalation

### Expiratory reserve volume:
EXTRA Vol of air during MAX expiration

### Functional Residual capacity:
Vol of air remaining after passive expiration

### Residual volume:
Vol of air remaining after MAX exhalation
RV = FRC - ERV
