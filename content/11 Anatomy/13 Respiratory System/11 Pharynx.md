## Function
- connects [[Oral Cavity]], [[10 Nasal Cavity|Nasal Cavity]] and [[Esophagus]]
- connection for solid food, liquids, air

## Subdivisions
### Nasopharynx
- connects to nose
- lined with [[Types of Epithelial Tissue#^ec17b6|pseudostratified columnar epithelium]]

### Oropharynx
- connects to mouth
### Laryngopharynx
- Connects to larynx
- food normally passes through oropharynx and laryngopharynx before reaching [[Esophagus]]
- lined with [[Types of Epithelial Tissue#^ec17b6|stratified squamous epithelium]]
