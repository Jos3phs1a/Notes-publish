### Nasal Vestibule
- contains skin and hair(vibrissae) that ==filters air==.

### Roof
**Olfactory epithelium** ^3ea3b3
- sense of smell, not useful in gaseous exchange

**Lateral walls: respiratory epithelium**
- Lateral walls lined with turbinates (bumpy tissue projections) → slow down air flow to aid in conditioning and filtering
- lined with [[Types of Epithelial Tissue#^ec17b6|pseudostratified columnar epithelium]] (pseudostratified ciliated columnar)
	function: **moves mucus** towards [[11 Pharynx|pharynx]] 

**Goblet cells**
	contain mucin, proteinaceous mix, **secretes mucus** to trap dust

**Lined with blood vessels**
	warms the air entering body for the lungs
