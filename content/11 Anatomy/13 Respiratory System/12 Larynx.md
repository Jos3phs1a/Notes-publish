
## Function
- prevent asphyxiation(choking)
- voice production via vocal folds

## Structures
### Epiglottis
- overhangs larynx to protect passage from obstruction
- prevents food from entering larynx and trachea → choking/aspiration
### Glottis
- can adjust passage for oxygen intake and preventing food intake by closing(adduction) or opening(abduction)
- [[12 Larynx|Larynx]] and [[13 Trachea|trachea]] can also narrow to create more space for food to enter [[esophagus]] 

### Cartilage
**Thyroid cartilage**(largest):
	also connects to [[thyroid gland]] (metabolism hormone producer)

**Cricoid cartilage**

**Arytenoid cartilage:**
	allow for movement of vocal folds via abduction and adduction
	also connects to thyroid gland(metabolism hormone producer)
