## Functions
- Shield from physical, chemical, biological
- Water proof layer (prevent loss/excess)
- UV radiation shielding
- Thermoregulation via sweat excretion
- Protection from external stimuli
- Creates vitamins and pigments e.g. vita D and melanin

- [[Dermis]] (inner): derived from mesoderm (connective tissue)
