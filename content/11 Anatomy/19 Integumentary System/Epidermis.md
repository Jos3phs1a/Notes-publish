### Stratum basale
1 layer of [[Types of Epithelial Tissue|columnar epithelium]]
sits on basement membrane via hemidesmosomes
mostly keratin stem cells ==melanocytes== & ==merkel== cells
### stratum spinosum
10-15 layers
Thickest layer of ==spiny keratinocytes==
Cells attach via desmosomes
### Stratum granulosum
3-5 layers
Cells Contain lamellar granules = lipids which are secreted to superior layer
Cells organelles start to disintegrate → make space for organelles
keratohyalins (mix of proteins) → bind keratin together
==bottom of waterproof layer==
### Stratum lucdium
Only present in thick skin, translucent layer → eleidin protein

### stratum cornerum
Outermost layer
Dead Keratinocytes lost all organelles → filled with keratin
Stick together → form cornified envelope

Note: keratinocytes found from [[#stratum spinosum]] layer onwards, more mature higher up in the layers + flatter
