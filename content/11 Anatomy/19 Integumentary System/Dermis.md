## Papillary Layer

Components:
- 'Peg’ like projections from epidermis
- **Loose Connective Tissue (areolar tissue)**
- allows diffusion of nutrients into epidermis
- Contains sensory receptors

## Reticular Layer
Components:
- **Dense connective tissue (Collagen) +** elastic fibres
- Contains blood vessels, glands, follicles 
- contains [[mechanoreceptors|receptors]] for deep pressure and vibration
