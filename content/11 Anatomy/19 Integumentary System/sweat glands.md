
## Function
- Secrete sweat (water + salt + urea)
- for Thermoregulation & waste/toxin excretion
## Structure
located throughout body, in [[Dermis]] layer but derived from [[Epidermis]]

### Merocrine/Eccrine glands
most common throughout body
innervation: acetylcholine
secrete thin sweat

**Mechanism of secretion:**
Release secretions via vesicles (packages of product)
### Apocrine glands

locations: Axilla, areola & genitals e.g. armpits, groin, eyelids
innervation: [[Adrenal glands|adrenaline]]
Secrete **Thick sweat** (contains additional protein components)
contributes to body odour
secrete via hair follicle.

**Mechanism of secretion:**
Pinch off portion of the cell containing products
Then repairs itself
