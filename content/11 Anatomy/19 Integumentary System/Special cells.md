## Keratinocytes
Location: [[Epidermis]]
Function: produces keratin
responsible for tough, protective property of [[structure of skin]], [[Hair & sebaceous glands & arrector pili muscles]], [[nails]]
## Melanocytes
comprise of 1:4 to 1:15 ratio in [[Epidermis#Stratum basale|basale]] layer, differ based on location of body

**Function:**
produces [[#Melanin]] then secreted into [[#Keratinocytes]]

#### Melanin
Function: protection against UV radiation primarily UV b
UVB STIMULATES SYNTHESIS OF VITAMIN D
Cannot penetrate the [[Dermis]]
**Transport**: sent to keratinocytes via melanosomes (organelles)

3 Types → composition of the 3 types affect skin colour

1. Pheomelanin : red to yellow
2. Eumelanin : brownish black
3. Neuromelanin: mix

**Function:** protection against UV radiation primary UV B

## Langerhans Cells
comprise 2-4% of epidermis
**Function:**
autoimmune detection
**Physiology:**
detect foreign particles **antigen** → migrate to lymph nodes → stimulate immune response in body

### Merkel Cells
locations: Basal layer + Hair follicles
**Function:**
sense of touch
endocrine function
