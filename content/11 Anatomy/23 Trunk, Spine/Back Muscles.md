# Intrinsic

ALL muscles in the group are considered deep, but are subdivided into 3 groups for categorization

**Superficial layer**: splenius (splenius capitis, splenius cervicis) 
**Intermediate layer**: erector spinae (iliocostalis, longissimus, spinalis)  **I Love Spine**
**Deep layer**: transversospinales (semispinalis, multifidus, rotatores)  **See My Rock**
**Deepest layer**: segmental muscles (levatores costarum, interspinales and intertransversarii)
