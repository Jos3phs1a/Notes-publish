## Prolapsed/herniated disc
Intervertebral disc bulges outwards, compressing Spinal nerves

## Epidural Anesthesia & Lumbar Puncture/Spinal Tap
Needles are inserted around L3 - L5, where spinal cord ends
[[cauda equina]] is present in this region, but nerves are mobile and harder to puncture

**Purpose:**
- **collection of cerebrospinal fluid (CSF)** in the [[Meninges]] to diagnose conditions like **meningitis, subarachnoid hemorrhage, and multiple sclerosis**.
- spinal anesthesia and **intrathecal drug administration**
