Consists 3 types of cells
resides near the [[Renal Corpuscle]] and [[Distal convoluted tubule]] 
## Macula Densa cells
macula = spot,  densa = dense
senses $Na^{+}$ concentration in filtrate in DCT
aids in regulation of [[glomerular filtration rate]] 


## Juxtaglomerular cells
Part of the [[Renin-Angiotensin-Aldosterone System (RAAS)]] 
Secrete renin in response to signals from macula densa cells or low blood pressure.

## Extraglomerular mesangial cells
aka mesangium
role in signaling between macula densa and juxtaglomerular cells.
