# Components

- Renal Capsule - outer membrane of the organ
- Cortex - superficial layer  
- Medulla - deepest layer
	- renal pyramids
- Renal Pelvis - area where collecting ducts connect
- Hilum - area where vessels for blood and waste excretion pass through

# Nephron 
smallest unit of kidney where most of processes leading to [[Formation of Urine]]  takes place 
2 types of nephrons
- Cortical nephrons ~ 80-85% contained in cortex region
- Juxtamedullary nephrons ~ 15-20% contained in medulla region
