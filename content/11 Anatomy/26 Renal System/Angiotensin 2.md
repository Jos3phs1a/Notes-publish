Function: 
- Trigger [[Autoregulation#Myogenic|Myogenic Response]](**mainly constriction**) from arterioles to ↑ [[glomerular filtration rate]]
	- Greater Effect on efferent arterioles
- Stimulate  [[Renin-Angiotensin-Aldosterone System (RAAS)#Aldosterone|aldosterone]] release [[Tubular Reabsorption]] of Na$^+$ and Cl$^-$, water and loss of K$^+$ at [[Proximal Convoluted tubule]]
- Triggers ADH/Vasopressin for water reabsorption at [[Distal convoluted tubule]]
- Stimulates Thirst
