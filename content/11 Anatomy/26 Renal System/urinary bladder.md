- lined with ==transitional epithelium== that ==stretches== as bladder fills
- Composed of **detrusor** aka ==smooth muscle== 
- relax and contract based on signals from stretch receptors
relax = detrusor stretch more → store more urine
contract = detrusor push urine out

## Innervation
innervated by pelvic splanchnic nerves which carry parasympathetic nerve fibres from spinal cord S2-S4
Stimulate contraction of detrusor muscles  + relax **internal urethral sphincter** 

**external urethral sphincter** is skeletal muscle (voluntary) innervated by pudendal nerve from spinal nerve, mix of sensory and motor fibres
