# Location

- [[00 kidney Anatomy|Kidney]] located in **Loin region**
- vertebral level **T12 - L3**
- anterior to diaphragm, psoas major, quadratus lumborum, 12th rib & ==11th rib (for left kidney)==
- [[ureter]] - **T10-L2**
	- crosses pelvic brim, anterior to SI joint, where ==common iliac artery divides== 
- [[urinary bladder]] in pelvic cavity posterior to the pubic symphysis



# Components

- Kidney
- Ureter
- Urinary Bladder
- Urethra
- [[Renin-Angiotensin-Aldosterone System (RAAS)]]
