Lined with [[Types of Epithelial Tissue|Simple cuboidal epithelium]] **without** brush borders 

Fine tuning of concentration of following substances:

- NaCl
- K$^+$ 
- H$_2$O
- pH regulators
	- H$^+$ & HCO$^-_3$ 


## Mechanism of ADH
- Hormone released, travels to peritubular capillaries near DCT
- diffuse only basolateral membrane of DCT, adhere to receptors
- binding activates cycleAMP(cAMP) messenger within cell
- creates **aquaporin channels** on *Luminal membrane* →  more H$_2$O can travel through
