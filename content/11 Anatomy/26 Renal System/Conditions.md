## Renal Calculi/Stones
produce pain carried by sensory nerves to T10-L2 segments of spinal cord
Referred to loin to groin pain


## Hyperkalemia
High plasma K$^+$ 
- causes cardiac arrhythmias
- ↑ excitability of neurons & skeletal muscles

### body regulation
↑ [[Renin-Angiotensin-Aldosterone System (RAAS)]] input, secrete K$^+$ 


## Hypokalemia
Low plasma K$^+$ 
- result of kidney disease, diarrhea, diuretics
- muscle weakness, failure of respiratory muscles + heart

### body regulation
no secretion of K$^+$ into collecting ducts

## hyperphosphatemia  
High PO$^{3-}_{4}$ due to poor excretion from kidneys, result from [[02 Chronic kidney disease]]

**Symptoms:**
bone and joint pain, muscle cramps, and itchy skin or rash
