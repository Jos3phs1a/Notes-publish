Hormonal system that regulates the amount of $Na^+$ and $K^+$  excretion/retention in the body

# Components involved

## Renin
secreted by [[Juxtaglomerular Apparatus#Juxtaglomerular cells|Juxtaglomerular cells]] 
Function: initiating the renin-angiotensin-aldosterone system
forms angiotensin 1 via conversion
## Angiotensin
Angiotensinogen: **inactive form** secreted by [[Liver]]
Converted to Angiotensin 1 by Renin in response to stimuli (low BP)
Angiotensin 1 → [[Angiotensin 2]] in lungs by angiotensin-converting enzyme (ACE)


## Aldosterone

# Stimuli

1) Decrease in BP at glomerulus
2) Stimulation of juxtaglomerular cells by sympathetic innervation
3) Decline in solute concentration detected by [[Juxtaglomerular Apparatus#Macula Densa cells|Macula Densa cells]]
