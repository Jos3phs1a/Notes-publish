## Glomerulus
a small tuft of capillaries between Afferent and Efferent arterioles
Capillaries are in contact with [[Renal Corpuscle#Visceral Layer|Visceral Layer of Glomerular Capsule]] 
	- contain fenestrations on the endothelium
	- ==forming 1st layer of filtration barrier==
	- [[Basal lamina]] of endothelium forms 2nd layer of ==filtration barrier==

## Glomerular / Bowman's capsule
area where filtrate is collected from the glomerulus and connects to [[Proximal Convoluted tubule]]
### Parietal layer
Basement membrane is lined with [[Types of Epithelial Tissue#^ec17b6| Simple Squamous Epithelium]] 
not involved in filtration process
### Visceral Layer
Layer is lined with [[Types of Epithelial Tissue#^ec17b6| Simple Squamous Epithelium]] as well as specialized epithelial cells aka **Podocytes**
contain extensions aka pedicels (==form 3rd layer of filtration barrier==) 
surrounding [[Renal Corpuscle#Glomerulus|Glomerulus]] 

### Glomerular cavity
Space between the 2 layers that allow filtrate to pass through


## Processes 
- Renal corpuscle main site of  [[Formation of Urine#Glomerular filtration|Filtration]] 
-  [[Juxtaglomerular Apparatus]] resides near the vascular pole of the glomerulus, regulates GFR
