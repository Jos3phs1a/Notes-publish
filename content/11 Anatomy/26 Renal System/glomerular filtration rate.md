
Definition: Plasma filtered/ unit time
Normal range > 60 ml/min
Kidney dysfunction <45ml/min

## Net filtration pressure
**NFP** = **Hydrostatic pressure**(~55mmHg) - **Colloid osmotic pressure**(~30mmHg) - **capsular hydrostatic pressure**(~15mmHg)

Hydrostatic pressure = pressure difference between afferent and efferent arterioles
Colloid osmotic pressure =  pressure due to [[Blood Composition#^4f10bd|plasma proteins]] not in Bowman's capsule
Capsule Hydrostatic pressure = Pressure from liquid in capsule trying to re-enter capillaries ^4bfc38

## Filtration Coefficient
1) Surface area of glomerular capillaries, ↑ SA → ↑ GFR
2) Permeability of interface between capillary and Bowman's capsule ↑ porosity → ↑ GFR
