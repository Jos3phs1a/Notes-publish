Lined with [[Types of Epithelial Tissue#^ec17b6| Simple Squamous Epithelium]] 
2 components

### Descending Limb
Only reabsorbs water
- going down the descending limb, concentration of solute increases, ↑ [[osmolarity]]
- Concentration gradient causes osmotic concentration
- water exit limb via osmosis
### Ascending Limb
Only reabsorbs salts, impermeable to water
- going up loop, osmolarity decreases
- tubule fluid high conc of solutes, creates concentration gradient
- NaCl is reabsorbed via **active transport**, water cannot follow
