when body is in constant [[Energy Balance#^4f7330|positive energy balance]] 
person is diagnosed to be obese through [[BMI]] 

## causes
Physiological, lifestyle, environmental factors

- sedentary lifestyle
- abnormal feeding behaviour
- genetic factors
- [[food intake#Leptin|leptin]]-signaling pathway dysfunction
- Childhood overnutrition
- Hypothyroidism


## Treatment
- Lifestyle modification
	- manage energy output ie ↑output + ↓input
- Drugs
	- ↓degree of hunger
	- alter lipid absorption
- Surgery
	- gastric bypass surgery
