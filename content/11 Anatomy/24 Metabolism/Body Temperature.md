- **Internal core temperature**: temperature of ==deep tissues== of body
- **Skin Temperature**: temperature of surface tissue
- can be measured at different areas of the body, ==some more accurate than others==
	e.g. oral, armpit, rectal, ear, temporal artery
	Rectal typically  higher than oral by 0.5°C

#### Variations in core temp
Vary due to different rates of [[heat transfer]]
- Ranges between 36 and 37.5°C varies about 1°C during the day
- women experience monthly rhythm in core temperature
- core temperature ↑ during exercise
- older people → cooler body temp
