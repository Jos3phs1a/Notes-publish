
**Definition:** Rate of energy expended by body doing [[internal + external work]] Energy expenditure/unit time 

#### Calories
==c==alorie basic unit of energy
Definition: Amount of energy required to raise temperature of ==1g of H$_2$O by 1°C  ==
==C==alorie/kilo==c==alorie = 1000 calories = 1kcal ^1da63f



## Methods of measurement
### Direct calorimetry
Measures total quantity of heat given out by body/unit time

### Indirect calorimetry
Measures O$_2$ consumption and calculate via equations
Chemical Equation:
$$Glucose + O_2 → CO_2 + H_2O \Delta H= 2840Kj/mol$$
roughly 4.8 kcal/L of O$_2$
