Body mass index used to estimate the amount of body fat by using your height and weight measurements
equation: $$BMI=Weight(kg)/Height^2(meters)$$
exists WHO guidelines and Asian guidelines because at the same BMI, Asians have a higher body fat %, leading to higher cardiovascular disease risk
e.g. at BMI = 27.5 WHO guidelines state pre-obese, but for asian population, same value is already obese and CDR = high
