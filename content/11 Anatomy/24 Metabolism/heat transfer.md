## Mechanisms
### Radiation
transfer heat via electromagnetic waves that travel through space
majority of heat loss from body is through radiation (~60%)

### Conduction
transfer heat from warmer to cooler object through movement of thermal energy between adjacent molecules
~ 3% heat loss
### Convection
transfer of heat via movement of air currents
~15% heat loss 
### Evaporation
conversion of liquids to vapor that requires heat absorbed form the skin ([[Pespiration]])
~22% heat loss from evaporation
