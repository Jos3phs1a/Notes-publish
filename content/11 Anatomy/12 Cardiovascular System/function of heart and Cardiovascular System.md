## Function
Function of CVS:
	Deliver oxygen and nutrients to tissues (lungs and digestive tract)
	Regulate body temperature

More specific to blood:
	[[Hemostasis]] (thermoregulation, lack of O2)
	Immunity against infectious agents (autoimmune system)
	Transport waste products to be excreted ≠ excreting waste products (kidneys)

Function of heart:
	To facilitate CVS by pumping blood around body

2 types of Circulation
	Pulmonary:
		Circulation to gaseous exchange with lungs
	Systemic:
		Circulation related to bring nutrients and remove waste to and from body
