
## Composition
Comprised of 4 tissues in different proportions:

1. Endothelium - simple squamous
2. Elastin Fibres - ==stretchy== tissue
3. Smooth muscle - for vessels to contract
4. Collagen Fibre - ==resist tension== without disruption/rupture
