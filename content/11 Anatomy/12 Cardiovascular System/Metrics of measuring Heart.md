##### **Heart Rate** (HR): 
Recorded in Beats/min
##### End Disastolic Volume (EDV):
vol of blood at end of [[Pressure and Volume changes during Cardiac Cycle#^26d4b9|Ventricular Diastole]]
##### End Systolic Volume (ESV) : 
vol of blood at end of [[Pressure and Volume changes during Cardiac Cycle#^26d4b9|Ventricular Systole]]

##### Stroke Volume:
Vol of blood pumped our during Ventricular Systole

$$SV = EDV - ESV (\approx70ml)$$
##### Ejection Fraction: 
Ratio of stroke vol to EDV

$$ EF = \frac{SV}{EDV}$$
##### Cardiac Output (CO):
Vol of blood pumped out of heart per min

$$CO = HR * SV (\approx5L/min)$$
