late:**<br>==Undergoing Preload:==<br>Volume of blood in ventricle causes muscles to stretch, leading to stronger contraction during systole<br>_________________________________________________________<br>**Atrial Diastole:** <br>9: Atrial muscles begin to relax, Pressure decreases<br>_________________________________________________________<br>**Ventricular Depolarisation + Atrial Repolarisation**<br>8:Impulse received by AV node, causing ventricular excitation==([[Electrocardiogram\|QRS complex]])==, Contraction begins after slight delay<br><br>==Atrial diastole ECG not captured== as electric signal generated much lower<br><br>_________________________________________________________<br>**Ventricular Systole 1st phase: Isovolumetric contraction**<br><br>9:Sharp increase in pressure<br>*==AV valves close(1st heart sound)*==<br><br>10: Isovolumetric Contraction: ==all valves closed==, n==o change in vol of muscle length==, pressure increases until match aortic pressure<br>_________________________________________________________<br>**Ventricular Systole 2nd phase + Ventricular Ejection**<br>12: Ventricular Pressure > Aortic pressure<br>SL valve open → blood ejected → Volume in ventricle decrease(14)<br><br>13:Blood pump rate > Blood drain rate at vessels -> Aortic pressure increase<br><br>15: end of ventricular systole: [[Metrics of measuring Heart#End Systolic Volume (ESV)\|End Systolic Volume]] achieved ~55ml ([[Metrics of measuring Heart#Stroke Volume\|stroke volume]] ~70ml), with left over blood remaining in ventricle<br>_________________________________________________________<br>**Ventricular Repolarisation**<br>16: T wave return from depolarised state<br><br>**Ventricular diastole - early**<br>17: Ventricles relax → pressure decreases<br>When ==ventricular pressure < Aortic Pressure==, SL *==Valve closes(2nd heart sound)==*(18)<br><br>Note: if SL valves do not close, backflow of blood occurs<br><br>19: Isovolumetric Relaxation: ==All valves closed== → no change in volume(20)<br><br>21: ==Ventricular Pressure < Atrial Pressure== → AV valves open<br><br>22: ==Atria pressure== increasing due to constant flow of ==blood from veins==<br><br>Cycle restarts | ![[Pasted image 20250201231437.png\| center \|600]] |
