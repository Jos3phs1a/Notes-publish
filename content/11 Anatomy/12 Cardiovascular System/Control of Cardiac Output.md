## Heart Rate Control

^ba240b

### Influence of [[Processes affected by ANS action]]
[[02b Autonomic nervous systems#Sympathetic|Sympathetic]] Activity: increases Heart Rate
[[02b Autonomic nervous systems#Parasympathetic|parasympathetic]] Activity: Decreases Heart Rate

Both ==active at all times==, one will be ==dominant depending on situation== and fluctuate

e.g. 
**Resting:** S & P both active, P ↑,  S ↓
**Running:** S ↑ , P ↓

## Stroke Volume Control
### Frank-Starling Law
Law states: Increase in force or tension in muscle fibre depends on extent to which fibre is stretched

↑[[Processes affected by ANS action#^883c6a|Venous Returns]]  ↑EDV → ↑myocardium stretching → ↑force of contraction(contractility) → ↑SV

### Intrinsic Control
- Extent of [[Processes affected by ANS action#^883c6a|Venous Returns]]
- Higher EDV by FSL
### Extrinsic Control
- Extent of [[02b Autonomic nervous systems#Sympathetic|Sympathetic]] stimulation of heart of [[Processes affected by ANS action|ANS]]
- Parasympathetic ==does not affect==
- Increases Contractility of the heart
