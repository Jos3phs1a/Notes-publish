
### Overview
Location: Cardiovascular Control Centre in medulla of brain

Function: Alter S&P activity
Which affect behaviour of the heart and blood vessels in the body

Other sensors present in the medulla:
	Pain
	Baroreceptors
	Chemoreceptors
	Respiratory Centre

**[[Metrics of measuring Heart#**Heart Rate** (HR)|Heart rate:]]** bpm of heart

**[[Metrics of measuring Heart#Stroke Volume|Stroke volume:]]**
	vol of blood heart ejects
	**Venous returns:** return of blood ==from veins to heart== (EDV)
	**Contractility of the heart:** how hard ==myocardium contracts for given preload== ^883c6a

**Blood vessels:**
	[[Regulating Blood Flow#Mechanism|Vasodilation/Vasoconstriction]] of vessels
	To increase circulation/blood resistance in some parts of body

**Release of norepinephrine:** 
	==Neurotransmitter + hormone==
	Can also ==trigger [[Adrenal glands]](kidneys)== to release epinephrine

**Respiration rate:** bronchodilation/constriction
