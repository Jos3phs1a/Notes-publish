## Gastric phase

--- start-multi-column: ID_yhp2
```column-settings
Number of Columns: 2
Largest Column: standard
```

- food arrives in stomach
- ↑ secretions of gastric juice
- Long phase (3-4hrs)
### Mechanism
- stretch & chemoreceptors triggered due to increased distention and pH(acidity decreases) ^1725b6
- send signals to [[Enteric Nervous system|submucosal & myenteric plexus]]
- [[GI hormones#Gastrin|Gastrin]]  is released to trigger [[gastric pits & glands]] + Mixing waves initiated by smooth muscle → churning
