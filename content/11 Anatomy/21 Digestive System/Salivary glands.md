## parotid 
located anterior to [[01 Head & Neck Anatomy#^v7KZSaH6|zygomatic arch]]
largest salivary gland
deliver saliva via parotid duct, inserting at masseter muscle & 2nd molar

**Secretion:** salivary amylase
## Sublingual
located under [[Tongue]]
submandibular duct runs into either side of lingual frenulum

**Secretion**: mucous → buffer + lubricant
## Submandibular
located under [[01 Head & Neck Anatomy#^DHdbFxrP|mandible]] within mandibular groove
insert via Whorton's duct into floor tongue

**Secretion**: mixture of buffers, mucin(glycoproteins), salivary amylase

## Saliva
70% submandibular, 25% parotid, 5% Sublingual
1 ~ 1.5L produced per day
#### Composition
- 99.4% water
- 0.6% electrolytes(Na)
	-  Na$^+$ , Cl$^-$, HCO$_{3}^{-}$   

#### Function
- Lubrication, moistening food and oral cavity
- maintain balance of [[oral flora]] 
	- HCO$_{3}^{-}$  buffer neutralize acid, 
	- lysozyme, salivary lgA for immune purposes
- Dissolving chemicals to stimulate taste buds 
- Digestion of starch and lipids (minimal)


## Conditions 
#### Mumps 
swelling of parotid salivary gland
viral infection
symptoms:
	pain
	fever
	headache

typically affect bilaterally

#### Salivary duct obstruction
similar to mumps but affects unilaterally
