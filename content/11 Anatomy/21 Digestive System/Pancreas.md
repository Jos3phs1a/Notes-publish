## Structure
4 parts

#### head
lies within C shape concavity of duodenum
#### Neck
Positioned anterior Superior mesenteric vessels
#### Body

#### Tail
passes between layers of splenorenal ligament

## Blood Supply
#### arteriole
Greater pancreatic artery & dorsal pancreatic artery, branches of [[Splenic artery branches]]
head supplied by:
superior pancreaticoduodenal artery ← gastroduodenal artery
inferior pancreaticoduodenal artery ← Superior mesenteric arteries
#### Venous
head: superior mesenteric branches of hepatic portal vein   
rest of the pancreas: pancreatic veins draining → splenic vein.

## Functions
1. **Endocrine**   
	control the ==glucose level== in blood → pancreatic islets **(islets of Langerhans)**, containing **B-cells** secrete [[Pancreatic Hormones]] into the bloodstream  + somatostatin
 ^381c60
2. **Exocrine**
	help in digestion by ==secreting digestive enzymes== → the **acinar cells** and the epithelial cells  
	that line the duct system, secrete [[pancreatic juice]]  into the [[Small Intestine]].
