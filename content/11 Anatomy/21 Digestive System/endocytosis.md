Process of Substances being brought into the cell from outside
3 types;

### Receptor-mediated endocytosis
1. Ligands in surrounding bind to receptors on surface of cell  
2. Entire structure bonds with proteins to form vesicles with clathrin coat
3. Vesicle is transported into cell
4. Vesicle merges into the exit site, clathrin coat dissolves, releasing ligands
