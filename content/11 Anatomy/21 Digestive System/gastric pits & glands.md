## Oxyntic gland (body & fundus)

**mucous cells:**
	secrete mucous, protect against acid injury
	stimuli: mechanical stimulation

**Chief cells:**
	secrete pepsinogen → activate into pepsin → digest protein
	stimuli: gastrin, [[histamine]] 
	 ^9fa035

**parietal cells**:
	[[Gastric Acid secretion]] + intrinsic factor 
		- activates pepsinogen
		- breaks down connective tissue
		- denature protein


**Enterochromaffin cell:**
	Secrete: Histamine → stimulates parietal cells
	stimuli: ACh, gastrin

## mucous cells

**G cells:**
	secretes: gastrin → stimulates parietal cells
	stimuli: protein products, ACh

**D cells:**
	Secretes: somatostatin → inhibits parietal, G & ECL cells
