Function remove feces stored in colon
 
## Action
- distension of rectum due to stimulation by stretch receptors
- muscle contraction with relaxation of internal & external sphincter muscles
- voluntary straining movements causing ↑ intraabdominal pressure
	- contraction of abdominal muscles
	- Forcible expiration against closed glottis

### Feedback loops
When Stretch receptors stimulated, 2 reflexes are triggered

#### Short intrinsic myenteric reflex
- Myenteric plexus in **Sigmoid Colon** mediate the reflex
- Internal sphincter relax
- series of localized peristaltic contractions & rectum to move feces

#### [[02b Autonomic nervous systems#Parasympathetic|parasympathetic]] /long reflex
- signals coordinated by spinal cord in sacral region
- mass movements in descending and sigmoid colon 
- further relaxation of internal sphincter

==Defecation always ends with voluntary relaxation of external anal sphincter==
if not we would be shitting ourselves
