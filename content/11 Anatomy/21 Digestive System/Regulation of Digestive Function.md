### Functions being regulated
- smooth muscles → [[peristalsis]]
- exocrine gland cells
- endocrine gland cells
### Regulating factors
- [[GI hormones]]
- Intrinsic nerve plexus (short reflex similar to [[spinal reflex]]):
	- [[Enteric Nervous system]] which is a branch of the [[Processes affected by ANS action]]  aka brain of the gut
- Extrinsic nerve plexuses (long reflex)
	- vagus nerve
- Autonomous smooth muscle functions

All stimulated by **receptors**
- chemoreceptors
- mechanoreceptors
- osmoreceptors
