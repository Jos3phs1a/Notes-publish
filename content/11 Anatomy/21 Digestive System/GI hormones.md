
#### Gastrin
↑ HCl, pepsinogen secretion +  gastric motility

### Secretin, Gastric Inhibitory Peptide (GIP) & Cholecystokinin (CCK)
↓ gastric motility + secretion

contribute to [[control of gastric secretion]]

| Hormone  | Trigger           | Function                                                                     |
| -------- | ----------------- | ---------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Secretin | acid in duodenum  | stimulate pancreas to produce HCO$_3^-$                                      |
| CCK      | chyme in duodenum | stimulate pancreatic enzyme secretion<br>                                    |
| GIP      | chyme in duodenum | inhibits secretion of gastrin<br>stimulates release of insulin from pancreas |
