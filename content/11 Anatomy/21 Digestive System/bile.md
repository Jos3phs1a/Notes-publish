
- Continuously secreted by [[Liver]]
- contains bile salts, cholesterol, lecithin, bilirubin
- does not contain digestive enzymes
- concentrated and stored in [[Gall Bladder]]

## Functions
- fat digestion & absorption
	break down large lipids into smaller pieces → increases surface area for enzymes
	Formation of [[micelles]] 
- Excretion of water insoluble substances
- [[Bile salts]] stimulate bile secretion
- CCK promotes gallbladder emptying & relaxation of Sphincter of odd/Hepatopancreatic Sphincter HPS
- Hepatitis & cirrhosis
