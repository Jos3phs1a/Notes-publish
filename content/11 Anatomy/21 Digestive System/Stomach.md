## Function and Physiology

#### Mechanical Digestion
Presence of muscle layers allow for mixing food into Chyme
	contributes to [[gastric mixing & emptying mechanism]] 
#### Enzymatic/Chemical Digestion
Protein digestion by Pepsin activated by [[HCL acid]] 
#### Reservoir for food
Presence of Rugae allow stomach to be distensible(expand) and store food up to ==4 hours==
also allows for controlled rate of releasing contents into small intestine
#### Absorption
takes in alcohol, sugar, salt, water & drugs
no nutrients absorbed here
