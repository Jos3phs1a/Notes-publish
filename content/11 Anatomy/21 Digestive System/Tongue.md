## structure

#### Oral part
anterior $\frac{2}{3}$ 

3 structures containing **taste buds**
	1. Fungiform papillae (most anterior)
	2. Filiform papillae
	3. Vallate papillae (most posterior)

**Innervation:** 
taste buds: corda tympani nerve, branch of CN7(facial)
tongue muscles: hypoglossal, CN12
#### Pharyngeal part
posterior $\frac{2}{3}$ 


## Functions
1. mechanical processing
2. manipulation of food
3. sensory analysis
4. secretion of mucins and lingual lipase


## Conditions 
#### Ankyloglossia
shortened lingual frenulum due to cut/birth defect → restrict movement

#### Tongue ulcers /  tongue lesions / discoloration
Indicate systemic disorder
affecting [[oral flora]]
