### Motility
- contraction of smooth muscle in digestive tract
- mixing + moving contents in GIT
### Secretion
- Exocrine & endocrine secretions
- mucus - along entire digestive tract
- saliva, acid, enzymes, bile, bicarbonate, hormones
### Digestion
- chemical breakdown of macromolecules into smaller absorbable ones
### Absorption
- transfer of digested material from GIT into blood/lymph
