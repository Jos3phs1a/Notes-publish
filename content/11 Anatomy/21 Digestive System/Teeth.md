## Conditions 
#### Gingivitis
food substances not cleaned properly harden → form **calculus**
lead to gum inflammation, swelling.
**Treatment:** scaling/cleaning
#### Tooth decay or caries
infection of teeth which can cause cavities
**treatment:** root canal treat
	removal of nerves → unable to feel biting sensation → porcelain cup to protect from fracture when biting
#### Wisdom Tooth
3rd molar may cause impacted tooth when it grows out
- may cause occlusal surface → mouth unable to close properly
- bacteria infection
**treatment:** removal
	does not affect mastication
