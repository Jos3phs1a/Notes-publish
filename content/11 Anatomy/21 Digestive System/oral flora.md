Community of microorganisms within the mouth

- **Bacteria**: bacteria unique to mouth, including Streptococcus, Actinomyces, and Neisseria
- **Fungi**
- **Viruses**
- **Protozoa**


Role
- Some oral flora are relatively harmless, but others can cause disease 
- Oral flora can cause tooth decay and periodontal disease if the balance of organisms is disrupted 

Factors that affect oral flora
- **Saliva**: The flow rate and ion content of saliva can affect the types of organisms that grow 
- **Diet**: The type and frequency of food intake can affect the balance of oral flora 
- **Oral hygiene**: Brushing and flossing can help prevent the growth of harmful bacteria
