## attachments 
1. Greater curvature of the [[Stomach]] by the **gastrosplenic ligament**, which contains ==short gastric== and ==gastro-omental vessels.==  
2. left kidney by the **splenorenal ligament**, which contains the ==splenic vessels==.  
• The spleen is surrounded by visceral peritoneum except in the area of the hilum.  
• The splenic hilum is the entry point for the splenic vessels, and occasionally the tail of the pancreas  
reaches this area.  

## Blood Supply
**splenic artery** →  gives [[Splenic artery branches]] allowing for subtotal splenectomy. It drains into the splenic vein then superior mesenteric vein.
