2 relevant divisions

| Feature               | **Submucosal Plexus (Meissner’s)**                                      | **Myenteric Plexus (Auerbach’s)**                                                                                                                 |
| --------------------- | ----------------------------------------------------------------------- | ------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| **Location**          | Found in **submucosa**, between the mucosa and circular muscle layer.   | Found between **circular and longitudinal muscle layers** of the muscularis externa.                                                              |
| **Primary Function**  | **Regulates secretion**, blood flow, and absorption in the GI tract.    | - **Controls gut motility** (peristalsis and segmentation).<br>- stimulated by [[control of gastric secretion#^1725b6\|stretch & chemoreceptors]] |
| **Neuronal Type**     | Mainly **sensory neurons** detecting chemical and mechanical stimuli.   | Contains **both sensory and motor neurons** for muscle contraction coordination.                                                                  |
| **Primary Control**   | Influences **glandular secretions** and local blood circulation.        | Controls **muscle contraction** for food propulsion.                                                                                              |
| **Best Developed In** | **Small and large intestines**, where secretion regulation is critical. | **Throughout the GI tract**, but especially in the esophagus, stomach, and intestines for motility                                                |
