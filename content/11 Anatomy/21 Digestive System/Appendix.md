## Appendicitis
Acute inflammation of appendix, if not removed leads to rupture resulting in [[peritonitis]]
most common in
age: older children, young adults
region: west, affluent societies

Symptoms: 
- acute, severe abdominal pain
- abdomen most tender at [[McBurney's point]], at base of appendix

Treatment:
- Appendicectomy - removal of appendix
