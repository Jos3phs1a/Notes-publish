
Function controlled largely by [[Small Intestine#Duodenum|Duodenum]] as it receives food directly

## Mechanism

1. Peristaltic contraction begins from upper fundus, travels down to pyloric sphincter
2. contraction becomes stronger at **Pyloric antrum** → ==sends chyme forward==
3. Pyloric Sphincter partially opens → ==small amt of chyme== sent through (amt of chyme sent↑ based on strength of contraction)
4. when contraction reaches sphincter, it closes → ==no further emptying==
5. Chyme hits closed sphincter, sent backwards aka **retropulsion** causing chyme to be mixed

**note:** [[vomiting]] ≠ retropulsion/retroperistalsis


## Regulating Factors

| Factors                                         | Mode of regulation                                                                                                                      | effects on motility & emptying       |
| ----------------------------------------------- | --------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- | ------------------------------------ |
| **Within Stomach**                              |                                                                                                                                         |                                      |
| Vol of Chyme                                    | Vol of chyme affects distension, affecting gastric smooth muscle excitability <br>+ act on [[Regulation of Digestive Function]]         | ↑vol of chyme → ↑motility & emptying |
| Degree of fluidity                              | Chyme must be fluid form to be sent out of stomach, bigger, drier requires more time to digest                                          | ↑fluidity→ ↑motility & emptying      |
| **Within Duodenum**                             |                                                                                                                                         |                                      |
| presence of fat, acid, hypertonicity,distention | triggers enterogastric reflex/release of enterogastrones aka secretin + cholecystokinin<br>too much undigested food, more time required | ↓motility & emptying                 |
| **outside digestive system**                    |                                                                                                                                         |                                      |
| emotion                                         | alter autonomic balance                                                                                                                 | ↓motility & emptying                 |
| pain                                            | increase [[02b Autonomic nervous systems#Sympathetic\|Sympathetic]] activity                                                                | ↓motility & emptying                 |
