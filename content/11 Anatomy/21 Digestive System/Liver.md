## Structure
### Surfaces
#### Diaphragmatic 
lies inferior to diaphragm. close to
subphrenic recess: 
	separates liver and diaphragm, divide into right and left via **falciform ligament**
	
hepatorenal recess (Morison's pouch): 
	part of peritoneal cavity on right side between liver and right kidney & suprarenal gland
#### Visceral
covered by visceral peritoneum except in fossa for [[Gall Bladder]]. all lobes can be seen clearer at this angle

**4 Lobes**

**4 Ligaments**

## Blood Supply
#### arteriole
right & left hepatic arteries ← hepatic artery proper ← celiac trunk
#### Venous
[[hepatic portal vein]] → 3 hepatic veins(middle, left, right) → inferior vena cava
#### lymphatic
anterior: Hepatic lymph node in lesser omentum
posterior: phrenic + posterior mediastinal lymph nodes
## Functions

#### Metabolic Regulation
Storage of:
- iron
- lipids
- heavy metals
- [[lipoproteins|cholesterol]]
- pathogens
- glucose (+release)
#### Hematological regulation
produce proteins for blood plasma
clearing drugs
regulate [[Hemostasis]]
#### Bile Production
emulsification of lipids for digestion


## Conditions
#### Cirrhosis
Liver damage causing fibrosis → hardening

#### Ascites
fluid collecting in peritoneal cavity
Body retains fluid because of portal [[Hypertension]] due to:
	[[Liver#Cirrhosis|Cirrhosis]]
	heart failure
	thrombosis
	infection
to combat:
		body forms varicose veins → [[varices]]
#### Jaundice
Bilirubin build up, precipitate into skin causing discolouration
