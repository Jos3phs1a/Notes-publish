
## Structure
3 components
#### Duodenum
- shortest section 
- 4 parts 
- 2nd part most important → location where [[bile]] and  [[pancreatic juice|pancreatic enzymes]]  enter
#### Jejunum
- 2.5m long
#### Ileum
- 3.5m long, connects to [[Large Intestine]]
- point where terminal ileum connects to cecum of colon → ileocecal juncture, contains ileocecal sphincter 
#### Muscle layers
similar to [[Stomach#^729bcf|stomach]] but muscularis externa only 2 layers like rest of the tract

Mucosa layer adaptation for [[Absorption Mechanism|Absorption]]
- Plica Circularis
- Villi
- Microvilli

## Physiology
functions:
	1. Complete digestion
	2. Selective absorption
	3. secretion of some hormones
	4. delivers chyme from stomach to colon

Main method to propel chyme along tract:
[[segmentation contraction]] 
faster in duodenum(12/min), slower at terminal ileum(9/min)
[[peristalsis]] used to clean intestines between meals
