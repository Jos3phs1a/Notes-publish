Most abundant hormone produced by [[Central Endocrine Glands#Anterior portion|anterior pituitary gland]]
## Functions
### Growth
one of the main factors of human growth
	- other factors include:
	- genes
	- diet
	- Freedom from chronic disease and stressful conditions
	- Levels of other hormones
		- insulin
		- thyroid hormone
		- sex hormones (androgens & estrogens)

Done indirectly via stimulating insulin growth factors (IGF)
	- Tissues that are responsive to growth-promoting effects undergo:
	- Hypertrophy: ↑cell size
	- Hyperplasia: ↑cell count

### Metabolic effects
Exert metabolic effect on body 
	GH is to mobilize fat stores while sparing glucose for glucose dependent tissues such as the brain
	via binding directly to adipose tissue, skeletal muscles & liver 
	↓ glucose uptake by muscles + ↑ glucose output by the liver mobilized fatty acids instead of glucose as a metabolic fuel
