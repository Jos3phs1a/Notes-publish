## Anatomy
- Lies inferior to thyroid cartilage of Larynx
- **2 lobes**, connected by **isthmus**
- Posterior aspect contains 4 parathyroid glands
- Major cells organised into **[[colloid]]-filled follicles**
	- [[Thyroid hormone]] synthesis occur on thyroglobulin molecules within colloid
- Interstitial spaces between follicles are C cells, secrete [[Calcium & Phosphate Hormones]]
