## Complexity of Functions

1. may be secreted by >1 endocrine gland e.g. somatostatin secreted by hypothalamus and pancreas
2. may have >1 target cell → >1 action e.g. Vasopressin(ADH)
3. target cell may be influenced by >1 hormone e.g. Liver influenced by insulin & glucagon ^cf217c
4. Rate of secretions may vary over time in [[Control of hormone secretion|cyclic pattern]] e.g. Melatonin, more secreted at night
5. May be hormones & neurotransmitters e.g. Noradrenaline
