
 Hormones that [[Classifications of hormones#^cf217c|act on same target cell]] simultaneously can result in different effects

### Antagonistic
opposite effects

### Synergistic 
Additive effects
2 or more hormones work together producing greater effect than done individually

### Permissive 
One is needed for another to produce effect i.e. gatekeeping

### Integrative
Different but complementary results
e.g. 2 or more hormones produced combined response
