**Bones store both calcium and phosphate** as hydroxyapatite Ca$_{10}$(PO$_{4}$)$_{6}$(OH)$_{2}$, which gives bones strength

hence phosphate and calcium homeostasis closely associated

| Calcium                     | Phosphate           |
| --------------------------- | ------------------- |
| 0.1% in ecf                 | < 1% in ecf         |
| 1 % in cells and organelles | 14 ~ 15% in cells   |
| > 98% stored in bones       | 85% stored in bones |

require delicate balance to maintain bones using [[Calcium & Phosphate Hormones]] 
to undergo processes:
 - [[bone deposition]]
 - [[bone remodeling]]

Inbalance leads to [[Calcium Metabolism Disorders]]
