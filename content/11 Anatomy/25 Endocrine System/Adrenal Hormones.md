## Epinephrine/Norepinephrine 

^2ee779

- Reinforces [[Processes affected by ANS action]] 
- ↑ heart rate
- have **different affinity** to **alpha beta receptors**
	- Epinephrine equal affinity to alpha & beta receptors
	- Norepinephrine higher affinity to alpha receptors
- when made in [[Adrenal glands#Adrenal Medulla|adrenal medulla]] → hormone
- when made in neurons → neurotransmitter
