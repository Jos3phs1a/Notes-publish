

Bone deposition is the process of forming new bones:

requires ↑ plasma Ca$^{2+}$ levels in blood via [[Calcium & Phosphate Hormones#Parathyroid Hormone (PTH)|PTH]] 
	- increasing action of [[Calcium & Phosphate Hormones#Osteoclasts|Osteoclast]] activity → result in dissolution of bone
	- Increases kidney excretion of phosphate, preventing it from binding to calcium and reducing blood calcium levels
