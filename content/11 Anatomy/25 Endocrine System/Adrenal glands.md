## Anatomy
- Located superior to kidneys

### Layers
#### Adrenal Cortex
- Capsule
- zona glomerulosa 
	[[Renin-Angiotensin-Aldosterone System (RAAS)#Aldosterone|Aldosterone]] 
- Zona fasciculata
	Cortisol 
- Zone reticularis
	sex hormones/androgens (both male and female in both sexes, different concentration)
	
#### Adrenal Medulla
- Consist of modified ==sympathetic postganglionic neurons==
- Epinephrine & norepinephrine are produced but in different concentration
	- Epinephrine → 75%
	- Norepinephrine → 25%
- Postganglionic neurons produces mostly noradrenaline, small amts adrenaline
