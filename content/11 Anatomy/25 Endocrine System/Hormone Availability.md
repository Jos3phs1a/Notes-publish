## Regulating Factors

### Rate of Hormone Secretion
more in blood → ready to affect

### Rate of Metabolic Conversion/Activation
conversion - break down 
Activation - triggering dormant hormones in bloodstream that are not presently used e.g. insulin/ Thyrosine

### Transportation 
more applicable to [[lipophilic hormones]] 

### Rate of removal by excretion of metabolic inactivation
hormones cannot remain in body constant and must be removed
