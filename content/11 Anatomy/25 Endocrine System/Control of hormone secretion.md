
### Negative Feedback control
Output of system counteracts change in inputs
e.g. hormone conc ↑ → endocrine gland ↓ secretion


### Neuroendocrine 
Fight or Flight responses


### Circadian/Diurnal rhythm or Time-based
Secretions are affected by time periods e,g,24 hour clock or menstrual cycle 


### other triggers
1. Humoral - change in ECF/ concentration in blood
2. Hormonal - arrival or removal of other hormones
3. Neural - stimulation by neurotransmitters
