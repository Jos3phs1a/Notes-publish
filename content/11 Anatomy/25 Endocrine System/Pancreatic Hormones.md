2 hormones work together to maintain blood sugar levels
Too much sugar → hyperglycemia/diabetes
Too little sugar → Hypoglycemia
## Insulin
- 2 amino acid chains
- connected via disulfide linkages
- when split apart, whole hormone loses functional activity
- Stimulates [[Metabolism of different organic compounds#Glycolysis|Glycolysis]] + ↑ conc of glucose inside **muscle cells**, ↓ conc in blood

## Glucagon
- Oppose action of insulin
- secreted during postabsorptive state
