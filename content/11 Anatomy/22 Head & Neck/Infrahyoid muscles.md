
## Anatomy
4 muscles in neck that depress hyoid bone:
TOSS
Superficial - Omohyoid + Sternohyoid
Deep - Thyrohyoid +  Sternohyoid

| Muscles       | Origin                                                                                                       | Insertion                                                                   | Innervation              | Action                                |
| ------------- | ------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------ | --------------------------------------------------------------------------- | ------------------------ | ------------------------------------- |
| Omohyoid      | Inferior belly: superior border of scapula near suprascapular notch  <br>Superior belly: intermediate tendon | Inferior belly: intermediate tendon  <br>Superior belly: body of hyoid bone | Cervical Plexus<br>C1-C3 | Depress hyoid bone Posteriorly        |
| Sternohyoid   | Manubrium of sternum, medial end of clavicle                                                                 | Inferior border of body of hyoid bone                                       | ^                        | Depresses hyoid bone                  |
| Thryohyoid    | Oblique line of thyroid cartilage                                                                            | Inferior border of body and greater horn of hyoid bone                      | ^                        | Depresses hyoid bone, elevates larynx |
| Sternothryoid | Posterior surface of manubrium of sternum, costal cartilage of rib 1                                         | Oblique line of thyroid cartilage                                           | ^                        | Depresses larynx                      |

## Function:
- aid in swallowing
- aid in speech
