acts as 
- shock absorber 
- diffusion medium for dissolved gases, nutrients, chemical messengers, and wastes
found in [[Meninges#Subarachnoid Space|subarachnoid space]]

## production
1. Produced in ==choroid process==(ventricles of brain) via ependymal cells
2. Circulate throughout ventricles exit via 4th
3. Flow in subarachnoid space
4. Reabsorbed into venous blood via arachnoid villi
