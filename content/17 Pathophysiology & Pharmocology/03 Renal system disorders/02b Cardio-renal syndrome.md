spectrum of disorders in which acute or chronic dysfunction of the heart or kidneys leads to acute or chronic dysfunction of the other.

- **Type 1 (Acute cardiorenal)**: idiopathic heart damage, leads to [[01 Acute Kidney injury]] or [[02 Chronic kidney disease]]. 
- **Type 2 (Chronic cardiorenal)**: Chronic issues with how well your heart works leads to [[02 Chronic kidney disease]].
- **Type 3 (Acute renocardiac)**: [[01 Acute Kidney injury]] causes acute heart failure.
- **Type 4 (Chronic renocardiac)**: [[02 Chronic kidney disease]] causes long-term issues with your heart, like abnormal heart rhythms, heart failure or [coronary artery disease](https://my.clevelandclinic.org/health/diseases/16898-coronary-artery-disease) (CAD).
- **Type 5 (Secondary cardiorenal)**: A disorder that affects your whole body makes your heart and kidneys unable to work well.
