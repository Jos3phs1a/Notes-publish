### Deceased donor
- standard criteria
- expanded criteria
	- older than 60y/o
	- 50-59 y/o meeting 2 criteria 
		- Terminal Cr>1.5mg/dl
		- Death due to CVA
		- Hypertension
- donation after cardiac death
	Death declared on basis of cardiopulmonary criteria rather than neuro criteria used to declare brain dead

## Benefits
- improved survival
- correction of metabolic abnormalities
- avoid complications due to [[05 Hemodialysis]] & [[06 Peritoneal Dialysis]]
- QOL
- ↓ healthcare costs

--- column-break ---

## Risks
- surgical risks
- rejection
- cancer
- infections - immunosuppression to reduce rejection
- cardiovascular disease
- Aggravation of pre-existing comorbidities
- toxicities of drugs
