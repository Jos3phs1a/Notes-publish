Reversible from of [[01 Acute Kidney injury]] occurs after administration of radiocontrast media

**Symptoms:**
- acute decline in renal function occurring 48-72 hours after intravascular injection of contrast medium without alternative explanation

## Risk factors
| patient related                | Procedure related                |
| ------------------------------ | -------------------------------- |
| [[02 Chronic kidney disease]]     | increased dose of radiocontrast  |
| diabetes mellitus              | multiple procedures within 72hrs |
| intravascular volume depletion | intra-arterial administration    |
| Reduced cardiac output         | type of radiocontrast            |
| Concomitant nephrotoxins       |                                  |
