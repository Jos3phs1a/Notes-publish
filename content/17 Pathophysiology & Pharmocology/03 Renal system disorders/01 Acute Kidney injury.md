# Classification
Defined as ONE of the following

- Increase in [[creatinine]] by >0.3mg/dl (26.5μmol/l) within ==48 hours==
- Increase in [[creatinine]] to >1.5 times baseline, which is known or presumed to have occurred ==within prior 7 days==
- Urine vol <0.5ml/kg/h for ==6 hours==

## Prerenal
**perfusion related**
sudden + severe ↓ in BP, interruption of blood flow to kidneys from severe injury or illness

**cardiac dysfunction**
- myocardial infection
- heart failure

**Vasodilation**
- [[sepsis]]
- cirrhosis 
- Anaphylaxis

**Hypovolemia**
- Hemorrhage
- Diuresis

**Vascular resistance**
- blood loss
- dehydration
- vascular occlusion

--- column-break ---

## Intrinsic Renal
**anatomy related**
Direct injury to the kidneys by inflammation, drugs, toxins, infection or reduced bloods supply

**[[glomerular filtration rate|Glomerular]]**
- Glomerulonephritis ^885c84

**Vascular**
- vasculitis
- Renal artery stenosis
- Renal vein thrombosis
- Prolonged hypotension

**Tubular**
- Acute tubular necrosis
- drug/toxin induced ischemia

**Interstitial **
- drugs
- Toxins

--- column-break ---

## Postrenal
**urine related**
sudden obstruction of urine flow due to enlarged prostate, kidney stones, bladder injury or tumor
- Benign prostatic hyperplasia
- cervical cancer
- metal stenosis/phimosis
- retroperitoneal fibrosis
- prostate cancer
- urinary calculi

**Intra-renal**
Nephrolithiasis
papillary necrosis

**Extra-renal**
Urothiasis
prostatic hypertrophy
