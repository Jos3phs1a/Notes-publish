- [[02b Cardio-renal syndrome]]
- [[02c Hepato-renal syndrome]] 
- [[02d Polycystic kidney disease]] 
# Pathophysiology 

![[Pasted image 20250922114811.png]]

TLDR; poor perfusion or filtration → damage to [[00 kidney Anatomy#Nephron|nephrons]] → [[Renin-Angiotensin-Aldosterone System (RAAS)|RAAS]] compensate, but worsens damage → further ↓ renal function 
thats why treatment often aims to reduce blood pressure or compensation from [[Renin-Angiotensin-Aldosterone System (RAAS)|RAAS]]
# Complications

### [[Blood Conditions & Disorders#Anemia|Anemia]]
- Poor [[Erythropoiesis process and factors#^1c038c|EPO]] production → ↓ [[Blood Composition#Erythrocytes|RBC]] production and life span (to 60-90 days)
- Uraemic toxins cause [[Function of platelets in Hemostasis|platelet]] dysfunction affects [[Hemostasis]]

**Treatment:** 
1. Iron supplements
2. SC EPO injections

### [[Acidosis|Metabolic Acidosis]] 
- [[00 kidney Anatomy|kidneys]] unable to produce ammonia in [[Proximal Convoluted tubule]] to excrete acid into urine as NH$^{+}_{4}$ 

**Treatment:**
1. Oral Sodium bicarbonate 
### Blood Pressure [[Hypertension]]
- refer to [[#Pathophysiology]], [[Renin-Angiotensin-Aldosterone System (RAAS)|RAAS]] compensation causes ↑ BP

**Treatment:**
1. Low salt diet
2. Pharmaceutical (ABCD)
	1. ACE inhibitors/ARB (preferred as anti proteiuric)
	2. Beta blocks
	3. Calcium channel blockers
	4. Diuretics
### Bone density 
- Low turnover, High turnover → ↑ fracture risk

### [[Heart Conditions & Disorders|Cardiovascular disease]]
- leading cause of death in [[02 Chronic kidney disease]] patients

**Treatment:**
1. Traditional factors. e.g. obesity, smoking etc
2. Non traditional factors: albuminuria
### Constitutional
non-specific indicators of illness due to toxin accumulation hormone, electrolyte imbalance e.g. 
- Pruritus
- Nausea
- Loss of appetite
### Diet
- malnutrition
**Treatment:**
- Low salt diet
- Low potassium diet
- Low phosphate
### Electrolytes
- [[11 Anatomy/26 Renal System/Conditions#Hyperkalemia|Hyperkalemia]]
- [[11 Anatomy/26 Renal System/Conditions#Hypokalemia|Hypokalemia]]
- [[11 Anatomy/26 Renal System/Conditions#hyperphosphatemia|Hyperphosphatemia]]

### Fluid 
- Failure to excrete sodium & watewr cause extracellular vol expansion and body vol overload

**Treatment:**
- fluid restriction
- Diuretics
- low salt diet
