# Strong regenerative capability

## Labile Tissue
 ==high rate of cell division== and continuous regeneration from stem cells 
 e.g.
 - [[Layers of skin|skin cells]]
 - Hemopoietic tissue
 
## Stable Tissue
tissue with ==low proliferative activity==, can undergo division if stimulated after injury
e.g.
- Parenchymal tissue [[00 kidney Anatomy|kidney]], [[Liver]] , [[Pancreas]]
- Mesenchymal cells [[Type of muscles#Smooth Muscle|smooth muscle]], vascular endothelium, fibroblasts

# Poor regenerative capability

## Permanent Tissue
cannot proliferate, 
e.g.
- [[Type of muscles#Skeletal Muscle|Skeletal muscle]], [[Type of muscles#Cardiac Muscles|Cardiac muscle]] heal by [[Hemostasis#^6e205b|Fibrosis]] 
- Nervous tissue heal by [[physiological effects of ultrasound#Cavitation|cavitation]]
