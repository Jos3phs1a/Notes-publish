
# Necrosis
--- start-multi-column: ID_prd3
```column-settings
Number of Columns: 2
Largest Column: standard
```

Cell death due to pathologic causes

e.g. Ischemia, bacterial infection etc.

**Mechanism**
- Cell swells up 
- Nucleus: pyknosis (clump) → Karyorrhexis (fragment) → Karyolysis (dissolution)
- Plasma membrane: Disrupted
- Cellular content: enzymatic digestion
- [[Inflammation]] present

# Apoptosis


--- start-multi-column: ID_s5pi
```column-settings
Number of Columns: 2
Largest Column: standard
```

Cell death but in a regulated manner, to remove old/unwanted cells
often physiological causes , but can be pathological
**Mechanism:**
- Cell shrinks
- Nucleus: Fragmentation into nucleosome-size fragments
- Plasma membrane: intact
- Cellular content: Intact, packaged into apoptatic body
- No inflammation
