## [[muscular conditions#Atrophy|Atrophy]]
Reduction in size of cellular content, but same no. of cells
due to:
- ↓ blood flow, vascular insufficiency
- malnutrition
- immobilization
- hormonal changes
- bone loss
- muscle wasting
- brain cell loss

## Hyperplasia
Increase in number
due to:
- ↑ functional demand in cells capable of dividing
- hormonal stimulation
- Estrogen ↑ uterus thickness for women/skin callus

## [[hypertrophy]]
Increase in size
- ↑ functional demand 
- hormonal changes
- [[Anatomy of heart, great vessels, mediastinum structures|heart]] & muscle hypertrophy
