# Types of healing
## Resolution

Mild-mod tissue damage, ==recover quickly== e.g. mild sunburn
- regain original structure after healing
- tissues capable of regeneration with intact extracellular matrix framework
## Regeneration

Damaged tissues in which cells are ==capable of mitosis== e.g. hepatocytes
- damaged tissues replaced with identical ones from nearby cells
- new replacement cells functional
- no [[Hemostasis#^6e205b|Fibrosis]]/scaring
## Replacement

For tissues that are ==unable to regenerate==, extensive damage or cells incapable of mitosis
- loss of function
- tissue replaced by ==connective scar tissue==
