
### Granulation tissue
- Temporary tissue, ==vascular in nature==, metabolically active with angiogenesis, appears ==pink/red==

**NOT SCAR TISSUE,** white pale + permanent 

### Collagen synthesis
- 3-5 days post injury, continues for weeks depending on wound size
- Increase collagen mass, cellularity and vascularity decreases

### Fibronectin & proteoglycans
Raw materials, 
- glycoprotein of [[Extracellular matrix]]
- scaffold providing tensile strength
- glute substances and cells together

### Elastin
- cross linking to form fibrils- provide elasticity to tissue

### Collagen
- provide structural support & Tensile strength
- key determinant of structural stability of extra cellular matrix
