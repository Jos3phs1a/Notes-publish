## Promote Healing
- Youth
- Good nutrition, protein vita A & C
- Adequate [[Blood Composition#^ed39ff|Hemoglobin]]
- Good blood circulation
- clean, undisturbed wound
- no infection/further trauma

--- column-break ---

## Delay healing
- Old age
- poor nutrition, dehydration
- Anemia
- Circulatory problems
- chronic disease or comorbidities
- Irritation, bleeding, excessive mobility
- infection
- chemotherapy
- prolonged use of [[Anti inflammatory medicine#Glucocorticoids|Glucocorticoids]]
