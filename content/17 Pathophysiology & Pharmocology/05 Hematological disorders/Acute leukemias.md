
# Acute lymphoblastic leukemia
- large number of blastic transformation of B and T cells in blood and bone marrow
- most common in children > elderly > adults
- aggressive, fast-growing
- fatal within weeks or months if untreated
# Acute myeloid leukemia
- Heterogenous clonal stem cell malignancy, immature ==hematopoietic cells== proliferate and accumulate in ==bone marrow, blood and tissue==
- greater than 20% myeloid blasts
- most common acute leukemia in adults
- most aggressive cancer with a variable prognosis, survival remains poor
- aggressive fast-growing leukemia with too many myeloblasts

### Acute promyelocytic leukemia
subtype of AML
reciprocal translocation between chromosomes ==15 & 17==
sensitive to ATRA (anthracyclines and aressnic trioxide) aka ==vitamin A → cure==
cure rate high
