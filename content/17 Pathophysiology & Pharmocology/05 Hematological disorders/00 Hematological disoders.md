
# Learning outcomes
1. Discuss the presentation, pathophysiology, diagnosis, and management of patients with acute / chronic leukaemia and their related complications. 
2. Discuss the presentation, pathophysiology, diagnosis, and management of patients with lymphoma 
3. Relate the above to your professional practice and your roles in the interdisciplinary care of patients with blood clotting disorders & neoplastic blood disorders

*many different blood disorders, only cancer- related ones are covered in this topic
