4 broad categories: 
generally: 
- suffix **-mia** → target immature cells, myeloblasts, lymphoblasts
- suffix omas → target mature cells e.g. lymphocyte, basophils etc

| Category                                                     | Origin                          | Example Diseases       | Nature                                 |
| ------------------------------------------------------------ | ------------------------------- | ---------------------- | -------------------------------------- |
| **[[Leukemias]]**                                            | Bone marrow & blood             | AML, ALL, CML, CLL     | Circulating immature white blood cells |
| **[[Lymphomas]]**                                            | Lymph nodes / lymphatic tissues | HL, NHL                | Solid lymphoid tumors                  |
| **[[Myelomas]]**                                             | Plasma cells in bone marrow     | Multiple myeloma       | Abnormal antibody production           |
| **Myelodysplastic Syndromes / Myeloproliferative Neoplasms** | Myeloid precursors              | MDS, Polycythemia vera | Pre-leukemic / proliferative           |
