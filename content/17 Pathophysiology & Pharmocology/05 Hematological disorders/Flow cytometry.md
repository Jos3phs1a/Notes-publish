technique used to diagnose [[Leukemias]]

- blood passed through laser beam
- Detects **light scatter and fluorescence** from labeled antibodies that bind to specific **cell surface or intracellular markers (antigens)**

uses:
- Cell counting.
- Cell sorting.
- Determining cell characteristics and function.
- Diagnosis and treatment of blood and bone marrow cancers.
- Finding biomarkers (a measurable substance whose presence might predict cancer behavior or response to treatment).
- Immunophenotyping (a way to classify cell types).
