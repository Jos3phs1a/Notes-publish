Usually a form of definitive treatment, with intent to cure

## Procedure
- complete resection most common and used for solid [[terms of oncology#Tumour|tumours]] 
Typically used for organs such as [[01ba Anatomic Divisions of Brain|brain]], [[01 Head & Neck Anatomy|head & neck]], [[15 Lungs|lungs]] , [[Esophagus]], [[Stomach]], [[Pancreas]], [[Large Intestine]]

Typically encompasses the following principles:
- resection of all tumour (to microscopic level)
- including regional lymph nodes - ==mode of travel for tumors==
- adequate margins observed
- en-bloc(all together) i.e. no fragmentation

### R0 resection
all of tumor removed with clear margins down to microscopic level
### R1 resection
incomplete resection, not clean not visible at microscopic level
### R2 resection
incomplete resection at a gross level, tumor still visible
