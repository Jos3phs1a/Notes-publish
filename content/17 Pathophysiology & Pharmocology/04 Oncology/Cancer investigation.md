# Radiological investigation

to see extent of local invasion, nodal spread, and distant metastases.
### CT & CT PET

--- start-multi-column: ID_4myv
```column-settings
Number of Columns: 2
Largest Column: standard
```

good for detecting advanced or bulky tumours
use of X rays to layer cross section image of body
- less sensitive for **early-stage cancer** or small polyps e.g. colon
- Tumors <1 cm may not appear on CT

**CT PET**
- most common is FDG PET -  radioactive sugar called fluorodeoxyglucose(FDG), identify areas of high glucose metabolism can be xcreted in urine
- Poor sensitivity for [[01ba Anatomic Divisions of Brain|brain]], [[Liver]], Urinary tract
