- can lead to abnormal appearance & behaviour

### Predisposing factors
- External
	- lifestyle e.g. smoking, diet
	- Environmental e.g. Chemical exposure, UV & ionizing radiation
- Internal
	- hereditary
	- age
	- infections
	- chronic inflammation: [[Liver#Cirrhosis|liver cirrhosis]], IBD

--- column-break ---

### Factors affecting cell growth
- proliferation
- cell death
- differentiation - loss of 
- Cell contacts
- extracellular matrix
- blood supply
- regulatory signals

--- column-break ---

### Genetic instability & mutations
- proto-oncogenes - promotes cell growth, stop functioning
- tumour suppressor genes - inhibit cell growth
