
## Cancer
- mass composed of primitive or dysplastic cells
- uncontrolled growth of cells
- leading to invasion of surrounding tissues
- ability to spread to other parts of body 
## Tumour
- uncontrolled growth of cells
typically ends with the '-ma' suffix
