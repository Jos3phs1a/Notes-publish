Treatment intent
## Radical
- Curative - eliminate the cancer completely 
	- e.g. - [[cancer surgery]]
- Mixed - chemo/radiation + surgery
	- Neoadjuvant - therapy then surgery
	- Adjuvant - therapy after surgery, reduce risk of recurrence
	
## Palliative
- relieve symptoms
- delay complications
- +/- prolong life

## Modalities
most can be used for either intent based on case
