## Hormonal Therapy
cancers like breast, prostate, uterine tend to be hormonally driven


### Mechanism
Cytostatic - refers to inhibits or slows the growth and division of cells without necessarily killing them 
stops cell proliferation, thereby suppressing tumor growth and spread

### Side effects
hormonal imbalance leads to 
- "menopausal" symptoms
- Cardiovascular
- osteoporosis


## Targeted therapy
uses drugs to precisely target and block specific molecules (like proteins or genes) involved in cancer cell growth and spread, while minimizing harm to healthy cells

target by

- structure
	- monoclonal antibodies
	- small molecules
- targets/action
	- tyrosine kinase inhibitors
	- anti-angiogenic
	- apoptosis-inducing
