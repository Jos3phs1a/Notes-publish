# Structure

S & P activity act through autonomic nerve pathways
2 neuron chains:

- Preganglionic:
	Cell body in CNS with axon extending to autonomic ganglion, connect via synapse(junction between 2 neurons)
	==CNS → Motor ganglion== (cluster of neurons out CNS)

- Postganglionic:
	Axon connect ganglion cell with effector
	==Motor ganglion → Effector organs==

### Sympathetic

Preganglionic:
	Origin: ==T1 ~ L2 Thoracic + Lumbar==
	Create ==network ganglia chain== at spinal cord, 1 preganglionic fiber can synapse with multiple postganglionic neuron → ==faster S response==

Postganglionic:
	Origin: Sympathetic ganglia chain/ collateral ganglia (halfway betw spinal cord and organ)
	==Pre fibres length < Post fibre length==

### Parasympathetic

Pre:
	Location: [[01a Anatomical divisions#brain|Brain]], Sacral region
	Vagus nerve ~ 75% P output for abdominal + trunk organs

Post:
	Origin: terminal ganglia (near effector organ)
	==Pre fibres length > Post fibre length==

Many organs have ==dual innervation (Both S & P)==
