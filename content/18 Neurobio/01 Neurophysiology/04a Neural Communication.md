Electrical signals are produced by changes in ion movement across the plasma membrane in [[01d Neurons]]

- Events trigger a change in membrane potential (triggering event)  
- Alters the membrane permeability and consequently alters ion flow across the membrane, causing [[04c Generation of Action potential]]

- Polarization: membrane potential is not 0 mv  
- Depolarization: potential becomes less polarized than resting potential  
- Repolarization: potential returns to resting potential after having been depolarized  
- Hyperpolarization: potential becomes more polarized than resting potential
