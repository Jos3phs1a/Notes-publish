## Receiving Sensory signal from [[01c Spinal Cord]]

Signal travel along Ramus → contain nerves from different regions
- Posterior: posterior skin + skeletal muscles
- Anterior: ventrolateral body surface, limbs
- Sympathetic nerve: internal organs

Unite at common pathway before spinal ganglion → end at dorsal/posterior root
→ end at respective sensor nuclei, somatic for external senses, visceral for internal

## Sending Motor Signal

From respective nuclei  → anterior/ventral root → split at common pathway
Travel same route as respective sensory input

**White ramus:** preganglionic (travels to [[02b Autonomic nervous systems#Sympathetic|sympathetic]] region to synapse w postganglionic)
**Gray ramus:** post ganglionic
