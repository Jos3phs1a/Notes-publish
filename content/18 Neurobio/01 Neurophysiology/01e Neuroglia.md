glia = glue

Do not conduct signals, but support function of [[01d Neurons]] and [[01a Anatomical divisions]] 

- **Astrocytes:** Control chemical content of ECF

- **Myelinating glia:** Insulation + speed of signal eg oligodendrocytes + Schwann cells

- **Microglia:** Immune functions, phagocytic

- **Ependymal Cells:** [[Cerebral Spinal Fluid|CSF]] production
