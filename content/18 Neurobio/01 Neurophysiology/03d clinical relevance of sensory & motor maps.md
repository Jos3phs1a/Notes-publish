[[03b Dermatomes]] → sensory loss pattern follows **skin segments** 

[[03c Myotomes]] → motor weakness follows **muscle groups** 

[[03a Sensory & motor homunculus]] → cortical lesions produce **regional body deficits** e.g. face, arm, leg that don’t match other distributions

| **Feature**    | **Dermatome (Sensory Root)**                          | **Myotome (Motor Root)** | **Homunculus (Cortex)**              |
| -------------- | ----------------------------------------------------- | ------------------------ | ------------------------------------ |
| **Pattern**    | Strip-like sensory loss (skin)                        | Muscle group weakness    | Region-specific (face/arm/leg)       |
| **Laterality** | Usually unilateral                                    | Usually unilateral       | Always contralateral (above medulla) |
| **Symptoms**   | Radicular pain, reflex changes,<br>Numbness, tingling | Weakness + reflex loss   | Sensory or motor deficits            |
| **Example**    | Herniated disc at L5 → big toe numbness               | C7 root → weak triceps   | MCA stroke → arm/face weakness       |
