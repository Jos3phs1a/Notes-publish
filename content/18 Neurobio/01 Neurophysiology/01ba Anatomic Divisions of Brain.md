# Internal anatomy
![[Pasted image 20250831221216.png]]

### Cerebral cortex
1. sensory perception
2. voluntary control of movement
3. language
4. personality traits
5. sophisticated mental events, e.g. thinking, memory, decision making, creativity, self-consciousness
### Basal nuclei
1. inhibition of muscle tone
2. coordination of slow, sustained movements
3. suppression of useless pattern of movement
### Thalamus
1. relay station for all synaptic input
2. crude awareness of sensation
3. some degree of consciousness
4. role in motor control
### Hypothalamus
1. Homeostatic function regulation e.g. [[temperature regulation]], [[Formation of Urine]], food intake
2. link between [[02b Autonomic nervous systems]] & [[Endocrine system]]
3. extensive involvement with emotion & basic behavioural pattern
4. role in sleep-wake cycle
### Cerebellum
1. coordinating skilled movement, balance, and posture
2. enhance [[Muscle Tension Properties|muscle tone]]

### Brain stem
1. origin of majority of [[#Cranial Nerves]]
2. Control centers for [[function of heart and Cardiovascular System|cardiovascular system]], [[00 Respiratory System|Respiratory System]], [[00 Digestive System|Digestive System]] 
3. Regulation of muscle reflexes involved with equilibrium and posture 
4. reception and integration of all synaptic input from [[01c Spinal Cord]]; arousal and activation of [[#Cerebral cortex]]
5. Role in [[Control of hormone secretion#Circadian/Diurnal rhythm or Time-based|circadian rhythm]]
