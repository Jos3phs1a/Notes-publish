- Describe [[01 Eye anatomy mindmap|eye anatomy]] 
- Describe structure of [[02d Retina]],  [[02a photoreceptor cells]], [[02b Bipolar cells]], [[02c ganglion cells]] and & their functions
- Understand [[03b Phototransduction|visual pathways]] to the brain
