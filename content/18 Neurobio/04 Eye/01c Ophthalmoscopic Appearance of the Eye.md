## optic disk
pale circular region 
- where retinal vessels are visible and originate from
- where [[01a eye anatomy#Optic nerve|optic nerve]] fibers exit the [[01a eye anatomy#Retina|retina]] 
- no [[02a photoreceptor cells]] here

## Macula
latin for "spot"
small, oval-shaped area near the center of the [[01a eye anatomy#Retina|retina]] 
- distinguished by the relative absence of large blood vessels
- mainly for central vision (as opposed to peripheral vision) → cones [[02a photoreceptor cells]]
