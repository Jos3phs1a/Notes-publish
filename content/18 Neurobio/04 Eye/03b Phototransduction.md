## Rods

**light conditions** 
Along disk membrane,
1. ==EM radiation absorption== by photopigment called [[03c rhodopsin]] in membrane of [[02a photoreceptor cells#Rods|rod photoreceptors]] 
2. ==Bleaching of rhodopsin== stimulates **transducin** (G protein) 
3. Activates effector enzyme **phsphodiesterase**(PDE), breaks down [[05b dark and light adaptation#^b70760|cGMP]] in cytoplasm of [[02a photoreceptor cells#Rods|rod]]  ^8a405a
4. cGMP conc ↓ 
5. causes ==Na$^+$ channels to close== and membrane to hyperpolarize
6. ↓ Glutamate release, affect [[02b Bipolar cells]]
