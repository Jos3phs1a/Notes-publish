regulates amount of light that hits  [[01a eye anatomy#Retina|retina]] for protection + visual acuity
controlled by [[02b Autonomic nervous systems|ANS]]

[[02b Autonomic nervous systems#Parasympathetic|Parasympathetic]] activity cause [[01a eye anatomy#Pupil|pupil]] to constrict
- sphincter pupillae muscles contract
- Dilator pupillae muscles relax

Vice versa [[02b Autonomic nervous systems#Sympathetic|Sympathetic]] activity cause [[01a eye anatomy#Pupil|pupil]] to dilate

# Mechanism

--- start-multi-column: ID_fvni
```column-settings
Number of Columns: 2
Largest Column: right
```

1. Light entering retina is detected by optic nerve, which sends inputs through optic tract 
2. synapse with pretectal area in the [[01ba Anatomic Divisions of Brain#Brain stem|brain stem]]
3. synapse with Edinger-westphal nuclei
4. send motor signals through oculomotor nerve to pupillae muscles
5. pupillar muscles contract/relax accordingly
