2 types of receptive fields but same concept applies

| Type                         | Center Response                                 | Surround Response                                   | Best Stimulus                               |
| ---------------------------- | ----------------------------------------------- | --------------------------------------------------- | ------------------------------------------- |
| **ON-Center / OFF-Surround** | Excited (fires more) by **light in the center** | Inhibited (fires less) by **light in the surround** | A **bright spot** in center + dark surround |
| **OFF-Center / ON-Surround** | Excited by **darkness in the center**           | Inhibited by **darkness in the surround**           | A **dark spot** in center + bright surround |


# Mechanism

#### OFF-center scenario
--- start-multi-column: ID_l039
```column-settings
Number of Columns: 2
Largest Column: standard
```

- bright center & surrounding → max inhibition
- dark center + bright surrounding → max excitation
- dark center & surrounding → mixed

response is determined by the fraction of the center
and surround that are filled by light and dark.
