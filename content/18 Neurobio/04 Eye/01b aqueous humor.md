
**Function:**
- maintain ocular pressure (eye pressure). 
	pressure maintained by having same amount inflow & outflow. 
	This fluid fills the anterior and posterior chambers of your eye.

1. formed by filtration from capillaries in ciliary processes((projections) within ciliary body
2. fluid flows from posterior chamber through [[01a eye anatomy#Pupil|pupil]] to anterior chamber, and some to [[01a eye anatomy#Vitreous humor|vitreous humor]]
3. Aqueous humor reabsorbed into venous blood via scleral venous sinus
		==failure to reabsorb fluid== leads to build up of ocular pressure →damage to eye → [[06 Eye disorders#Glaucoma|glaucoma]]
