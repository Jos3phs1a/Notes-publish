## Factors

- dilation of the pupils allows more light to enter the eye.
- restoring [[03c rhodopsin]] to unbleached state 
- adjustment of functional circuitry of  [[01a eye anatomy#retina|retina]] so information from more [[02a photoreceptor cells#Rods|rods]] is available to each [[02c ganglion cells]]

#### In dark conditions:
- cyclic guanosine monophosphate (cGMP) ==conc ↑== → cell remains depolarized due to Ca$^{2+}$ Na$^{+}$ flow ^b70760
- [[02a photoreceptor cells]] depolarized
- once Ca$^{2+}$, high enough → inhibits enzyme **guanylyl cyclase** that ==synthesizes cGMP== 
- **result:** cGMP levels high & stable ==(negative feedback loop)==

#### In light conditions
- cGMP-gated channels close, flow of Ca$^{2+}$ Na$^{+}$ into photoreceptor disrupted
- **guanylyl cyclase** → cGMP production increases BUT is overpowered by release of [[03b Phototransduction#^8a405a|PDE]] 
- **result:** overall cGMP concentration ↓
