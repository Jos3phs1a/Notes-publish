# Learning outcomes

- Define [[01b Pain definition|pain and its components]]
- Understand and describe [[Nociceptors]] 
- Explain the afferent innervations of the spinal cord
- Describe the ascending nociceptive pathways
- Apply the knowledge on ascending nociceptive & touch pathways on spinal cord lesions
- Describe the representation of pain in the brain
- Explain how pain can be modulated by touch
