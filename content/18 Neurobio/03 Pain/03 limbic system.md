### Parahippocampal Gyrus
Allows anticipation or recall of pain in specific situations

### Prefrontal Cortex
Provides top-down regulation of emotional pain responses. 
_increase or suppress_ the emotional impact of pain.

### Amygdala
Adds emotional intensity and forms _conditioned fear memories_ of painful experiences.
