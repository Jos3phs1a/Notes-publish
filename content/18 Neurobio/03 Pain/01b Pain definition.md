# Definition
An unpleasant sensory and emotional experience associated with or resembling that associated with actual or potential tissue damage

- not inferred solely from [[01a Nociception]], involve processing at the [[01ba Anatomic Divisions of Brain|Brain]]  
- is a subjective experience, varying by ==biological, psychological and social factors==


# Components

## Sensory
the objective component, involving noxious stimulus
- location
- intensity
- quality
- duration

## Affective
the emotional component
- association of aversive & negative feelings of pain and drive to seek relief
- depends on emotional state of individual
## Cognitive
the environmental component
- influence on pain by [[01a Anatomical divisions#1. Central Nervous System(CNS)|CNS]] or cognitive activities e.g. high stress environment feel less pain
