# signal pathway


![[Pasted image 20251011114920.png]]


1. enters brain through lateral pons with the trigeminal nerve
2. descend in the spinal trigeminal tract synapse on ipsilateral spinal trigeminal nucleus with 2nd order neurons
3. Secondary sensory neurons from the spinal trigeminal nucleus cross the brainstem to ascend as the trigeminothalamic tract
4. Trigeminothalamic tract fibers synapse in the thalamic ventral posterior medial nucleus
5. neurons project to regions of [[somatosensory cortex]]
