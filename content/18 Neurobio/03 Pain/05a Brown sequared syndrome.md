involves hemisection of the [[01c Spinal Cord|spinal cord]] (lesion on one side)

results in sensory and motor deficits

## Symptoms
### Interruption of [[02a Anterolateral pathways]]
- contralateral loss of pain and temp sensation 
- occurs 2-3 segments below level of lesion, discussed in [[#possible pathophysiology]]

### Interruption of [[02a Dorsal column medial lemniscal pathway]]
- Ipsilateral loss of tactile discrimination below level of lesion

#### Example
Damage to right side spinal cord results in 

| Left foot               | Right foot               |
| ----------------------- | ------------------------ |
| Touch sensation intact  | Touch sensation impaired |
| Pain sensation impaired | Pain sensation intact    |
