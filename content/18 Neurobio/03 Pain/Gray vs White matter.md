
| Feature           | **Gray Matter**                                                                                                            | **White Matter**                                                                                                    |
| ----------------- | -------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- | ------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| **Composition**   | Contains **neuron cell bodies**, dendrites, and unmyelinated axons                                                         | Made up mostly of **myelinated axons** (nerve fibers) that form tracts                                              |
| **Color Reason**  | Appears grey due to the lack of myelin                                                                                     | Appears white because myelin is fatty and light-colored                                                             |
| **Main Function** | **Processing** and **integration** of information                                                                          | **Transmission** of information to and from the [[01ba Anatomic Divisions of Brain]] and between [[01c Spinal Cord\|spinal cord]] segments |
| **TLDR**          | **site of detection and initial processing** of afferent signals — this is where sensory neurons make their first synapse. | **carries** the processed sensory information **upwards to the brain** through ascending tracts.                    |
