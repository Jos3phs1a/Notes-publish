
[[02a Dorsal column medial lemniscal pathway]] and [[02a Anterolateral pathways]]

have several differences:

|                                   Pathway                                   |                                                                       DCML                                                                       |                                                                                      AL                                                                                       |
| :-------------------------------------------------------------------------: | :----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------: | :---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------: |
|                              Signals conveyed                               |                                                    fine touch<br>vibration<br>proprioception                                                     |                                                                  Nociception<br>Temperature<br>Coarse touch                                                                   |
|                                   Neurons                                   |                                                     large diameter, myelinated A-beta fibers                                                     | more contribution of small diameter, unmyelinated [[Nociceptors#C fibers\|C fibers]]<br>with some contribution from myelinated [[Nociceptors#A-delta fibers\|A-delta fibers]] |
