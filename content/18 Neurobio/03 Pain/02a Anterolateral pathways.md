Consists of 3 paths:
- Spinothalamic - mediates discriminative aspects of pain and temperature sensation, such as location and intensity of the stimulus.
- Spinomesencephalic
	- mainly from spinal cord [[laminae]] I and V
- Spinoreticular
	-  arises diffusely from in￾termediate zone and ventral horn laminae 6 through 8

mainly involve Smaller-diameter and unmyelinated axons aka [[Nociceptors#C fibers|C fibers]] and [[Nociceptors#A-delta fibers|A delta fibers]] 
### 1st order
- signals enter through ipsilateral dorsal horn
e.g. left leg signal enter left dorsal horn
### 2nd order
- 1st order ==synapse within the dorsal horn== at corresponding [[laminae]]
- 2nd order axons decussate, cross through spinothalamic tract on contralateral side then ascend spinal cord

### 3rd order
- ==synapse onto 3rd order in Ventral posterior nucleus of thalamus== on contralateral side ^cc2692
- project onto corresponding regions of [[somatosensory cortex]] 

ventral posterior = lower part of posterior thalamus
