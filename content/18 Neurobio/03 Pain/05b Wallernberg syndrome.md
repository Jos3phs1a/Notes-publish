## Symptoms
- Difficulty swallowing
- Hoarse voice
- Hiccups
- Loss of sense of taste
- Fast, involuntary eye movements nystagmus
- Double vision
- Issues with balance and coordination
- Dizziness and vertigo
- Pain or numbness
## Pathophysiology
Lesion in lateral medulla due to ==occlusion of posterior inferior cerebellar artery (PICA)== comdue to stroke
affecting opposite sides due to differing synapse points of [[02 Ascending pathways]]
