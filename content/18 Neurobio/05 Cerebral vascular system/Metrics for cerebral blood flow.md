### Cerebral perfusion pressure (CPP)
[[Blood Pressure#^4ad161|MAP]]-(intercranial pressure)ICP=CPP
normal CPP is between 50-150 mmHg
- <50mmHg → ischemia
- >150mmHG → Hyperemia (risk rupture)

### Cerebral blood flow
$$CBF=Cerebral\,Blood\,Vol/time$$
- 750ml/min = 15% [[Control of Cardiac Output|Cardiac output]] or 2% body weight
- normal range: 
	- 45-50ml/100g/min
	- 20ml/100g/min in [[Gray vs White matter|white matter]]
	- 70ml/100g/min in [[Gray vs White matter|grey matter]]
- fall to less than 10-23ml/100g/min, physiological electrical function of cell begins to fail aka ischemia penumbra
- irreversible cell death → 8ml/100g/min

undergoes [[CBF autoregulation|autoregulation]] to main proper perfusion
