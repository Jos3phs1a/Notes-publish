Pathway for [[mechanoreceptors]] to transfer sensations of **fine touch. vibration, pressure, proprioception** towards  [[01ba Anatomic Divisions of Brain#Cerebral cortex|cerebral cortex]].  

Signals from different regions travel through the spinal cord through different portions of the [[01c Spinal Cord|spinal cord]], represented through a ![[topographical organization of spinal cord]] 
 

# order of travel

### 1st order neuron
Signals from [[mechanoreceptors]] sent up [[01c Spinal Cord|spinal cord]] through axons until they reach cervical region.
- travel along ipsilateral dorsal column ie right lower body signal travel on right side gracile fasciculus
- No synapses occurring → axons are very long, ~ 1.6-1.8m

### 2nd order neuron
At **dorsal column nuclei**, ==between the medulla and spinal cord==. ^52c692
- 1st order synapse with 2nd order
- Axons ==decussate== (cross each other, form an X) to medial lemniscus, and cross on contralateral side of medulla to brain.
- Ascend in ==white matter tract== aka **medial lemniscus** through medulla, pons, midbrain

### 3rd order neurons
2nd order axons synapse with 3rd order neurons in ==ventral posterior nucleus== of contralateral [[01ba Anatomic Divisions of Brain#Thalamus|thalamus]] 
- signals sent to corresponding areas based on [[03a Sensory & motor homunculus|sensory homunculus]] in ==primary somatosensory cortex, S1==
