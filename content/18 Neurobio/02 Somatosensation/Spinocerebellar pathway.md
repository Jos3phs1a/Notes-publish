# order of travel

### 1st to 2nd
1st order axons synapse onto 2nd order neurons in the dorsal horn of spinal cord

### 2nd order 
Neurons split into the dorsal and ventral tract, taking separate pathways to reach the [[01ba Anatomic Divisions of Brain#Cerebral cortex|cerebellar cortex]]

### Anterior tract
- sends information from ==L3 and below==
- ==decussate== & ascends contralaterally through the superior cerebellar peduncle
- 3rd order neuron will ==cross over again== to ipsilateral side

### Posterior tract
- sends information from ==C8 - L3==
- ascends ipsilaterally through inferior cerebellar peduncle
