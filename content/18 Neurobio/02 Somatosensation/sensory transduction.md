- process by which any environmental stimulus causes an electrical response in a sensory receptor
- involves the influx of ions across channels

## Types of receptors

- [[mechanoreceptors]]
	- e.g. touch, tactile
- [[chemoreceptors]] 
	- e.g. smell, taste
- photoreceptors
	- e.g. eyes
- Thermoreceptors
	- e.g. temp
- [[Nociceptors]] 
	- noxious stimulus
