Sensory system that supplies somatic sensation of the face & head

# Order of travel

### 1st order neurons
- [[mechanoreceptors]] mostly supplied by [[11 Anatomy/22 Head & Neck/01 Head & Neck Anatomy.md#^group=Wr1HCnYWGEZ5uhCrMS8S7|Trigeminal nerve (CN V)]] 
- enter [[01ba Anatomic Divisions of Brain|brain]] from the pons

### 2nd order neurons
1st order axons synapse onto 2nd order neurons in ipsilateral trigeminal nucleus, similar to [[02a Dorsal column medial lemniscal pathway#^52c692|dorsal column nucleus]] along brainstem
- axons decussate
### 3rd order neurons
2nd order synapse onto 3rd order neurons in ==ventral posterior nucleus== of contralateral [[01ba Anatomic Divisions of Brain#Thalamus|thalamus]] 
- signals sent to corresponding areas based on [[03a Sensory & motor homunculus|sensory homunculus]] in ==primary somatosensory cortex, S1==
