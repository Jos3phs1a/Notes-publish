To prevent loss of acuity in sensation due to overlapping [[signal encoding#Receptive field|receptive fields]] 
To enhance contrast of stimulus
# Mechanism 

1. neurons that receive strong stimulus, send out [[signal encoding#Intensity|stronger signals]] vice versa for weak stimulus in adjacent [[signal encoding#Receptive field|receptive fields]]

2. afferent neurons will synapse with adjacent neurons to send inhibitory signals to adjacent neurons, blocking out APs in adjacent [[signal encoding#Receptive field|receptive fields]].

3. Adjacent neurons send weak APs to [[01a Anatomical divisions#1. Central Nervous System(CNS)|CNS]], creating contrast
