Found within the [[Dermis]] layer of skin, are able to convert mechanical forces into ionic current via [[sensory transduction]] 

# Mechanism

### Direct opening
--- start-multi-column: ID_lcmi
```column-settings
Number of Columns: 2
Largest Column: standard
```

Forces cause ==membrane to stretch or bend==, causing ion channels to open
these forces can also alter gating and ==enhance/decrease channel opening==

# Types of mechanoreceptors

![[Pasted image 20250908012823.png|center]]


## Superficial 
--- start-multi-column: ID_roof
```column-settings
Number of Columns: 2
Largest Column: standard
```

### Meissners Corpuscle
location: papillary dermis, glabrous skin
Function: tactile shape/surfaces  ie light touch, fine textures

--- column-break ---

### Merkel discs
location: epidermis
Function: tactile: indentations ie light touch, deep static, subtle texture

--- end-multi-column
## Deep

--- start-multi-column: ID_264u
```column-settings
Number of Columns: 2
Largest Column: standard
```

### Pacinian Corpuscle
Location: reticular dermis and sometimes in hypodermis, mostly found in fingers
Function: vibration


--- column-break ---

### Ruffini endings
Location: reticular dermis, some joints & ligaments
Function: sustained touch, pressure
