## Temporal Summation

when a ==single presynaptic neuron== fires ==multiple action potentials== in rapid succession to reach threshold potential of the *postsynaptic neuron*

each EPSP adds on to previous one before it fully decays

--- column-break ---

## Spatial Summation

when ==_two or more presynaptic neurons_== fire action potentials together to reach threshold potential of the *postsynaptic neuron*

EPSP from different sources combine at [[01d Neurons#^bb7c6f|axon hillock]] 

--- end-multi-column
**Note:** 
- EPSP and IPSP are **Graded potentials!**
- Action potentials from presynaptic neurons **do not add directly** in the postsynaptic neuron.
- Membrane potential must rise within a short time frame, if not no summation will occur
- [[05 Type of Neurotransmitters#^9eef99|IPSP]] decrease membrane potential and can negate summation
