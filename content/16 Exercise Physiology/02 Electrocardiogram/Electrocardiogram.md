
## Overview
ECG used to measure electrical activity of heart at surface of body
- DOES NOT measure MECHANICAL ACTIVITY
- Not a reflection of electrical activity within the heart as come from deep within muscles, hard to detect

Allow for multi perspective view of heart

Setup of ECG:
	==12 leads==: 6 limb leads, 6 chest leads (lead = view)
	==10 Electrodes==: 4 limbs + 6 leads
	
Relationship of leads to view of heart
	**I**nferior wall: Leads II, III, aV**F**
	**A**nterior Wall: Leads V**1** to V**4**
	**L**ateral Wall: Leads 1, aV**L**, v5, v6

Acronym: IF,  A14, LL
