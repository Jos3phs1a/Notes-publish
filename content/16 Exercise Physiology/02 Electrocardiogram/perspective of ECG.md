### Frontal plane
Impulses typically travel from 11o clock to 5 
and towards left side of heart on transverse plane
so for frontal plane leads, 
==upwards → negative deflection 
downwards → positive deflection==

### Transverse plane
left side of heart more mass, impulses predominantly move to left side of heart 
depolarization is spreading away from a right ventricular lead outweighing those in the smaller right ventricle. In a left ventricular lead there is an upward deflection (R wave) as the ventricular muscle is depolarized
==Towards RV (v1,v2,v3) → negative deflection
Towards LV (v4,v5,v6) → positive deflection==
