[[Type of muscle fibers|Muscle fibers]] innervated by a single motor neuron


Recruitment of motor units typically vary based on demand of the task e.g. amount, duration of force during [[muscle contraction]] 

To be most efficient:
- Smallest to Largest unit
- Least fatigable(type 1) to most fatigable(type 2)
