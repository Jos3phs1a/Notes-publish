Lactate is the product of pyruvic acid when it's reduced during [[Forms of metabolism#Glycolysis|Glycolysis]] 
Build up of lactate will result in muscle fatigue in body up to and beyond [[Lactate threshold]]
## removal 
During recovery phase

~70% of lactate oxidized to pyruvate that can be recycled to produce ATP via [[Forms of metabolism#Oxidative Phosphorylation|Aerobic Respiration]] or [[Forms of metabolism#Glycolysis|Anaerobic Respiration]]  
~20% converted to glucose
~10% converted to amino acids

research shown that light exercise post exercise removes lactate quicker as O$_2$ is brought in at higher rate to reduce lactate to pyruvate 
## Terminology
some textbooks use *lactate* and *lactic acid* interchangeably but not the same depending on institution
