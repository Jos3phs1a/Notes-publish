# Energy demand on heart



#### Rate pressure product
quantity to measure oxygen consumption which factors both [[Blood Pressure|systolic BP]] &  [[Metrics of measuring Heart#**Heart Rate** (HR)|Heart Rate]] 

$$RPP= SBP\:*\:HR$$

| Hemodynamic Response | RPP         |
| -------------------- | ----------- |
| High                 | > 30 000    |
| High intermediate    | 25 - 30 000 |
| Intermediate         | 20 - 25 000 |
| Low intermediate     | 15 - 20 000 |
| Low                  | 10 - 15 000 |
