# Resistance Exercises

Response differs based on whether exercise is 
1) [[Static exercise]]
2) [[Dynamic resistance exercise]]

Also induces [[Pressor Reflex]] causing ↓ in [[Blood Pressure#^90fc31|SBP]] & [[Blood Pressure#^ddc4da|DBP]]

## CVS

--- start-multi-column: ID_70sa
```column-settings
Number of Columns: 2
Largest Column: standard
```

- ↓ [[Metrics of measuring Heart#Stroke Volume|SV]] due to High intrathoracic pressure compressing vena cava → ↓ Preload, ↑ Afterload 
- Large ↑ [[Metrics of measuring Heart#**Heart Rate** (HR)|HR]] to maintain CO
- ↓ [[Total peripheral resistance|TPR]] due to vasodilation increasing muscle blood flow, overriding ↑BP due to [[03a Valsalva maneuver]]

### Relationship with increased repetitions

--- start-multi-column: ID_xe4m
```column-settings
Number of Columns: 2
Largest Column: standard
```

- [[Blood Pressure#^4ad161|MAP]] and [[Metrics of measuring Heart#**Heart Rate** (HR)|HR]] ↑ with each rep performed
	But BP fluctuates more due to changes responding much faster than HR (does not fluctuate, nice positive drift)
- due to compression of blood vessels and [[03a Valsalva maneuver]] 
- [[Total peripheral resistance|TPR]] much higher during [[Dynamic resistance exercise]] vs aerobic exercise due to vasoconstriction brought about by [[Pressor Reflex]]
