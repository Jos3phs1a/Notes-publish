#### Phase 1
- Thoracic aorta compression → ↑ [[Blood Pressure#^4ad161|MAP]] 
- Less Venous Returns → Reduced filling and preload leads to ↓ CO
- Result: ↓ HR
#### Phase 2
- ↓ [[Metrics of measuring Heart#Cardiac Output (CO)|CO]] activates baroreceptor reflex → ↓ [[Blood Pressure#^4ad161|MAP]]  to compensate lack of blood flow → ↑ CO
- Result: ↑ HR

--- column-break ---

#### Phase 3
- breathing returns to normal
- Compression on aorta removed → ↓ [[Blood Pressure#^4ad161|MAP]] → ↑ CO
- [[Metrics of measuring Heart#**Heart Rate** (HR)|HR]] ↑ by reflex

#### Phase 4
- CO sudden ↑ due to rapid filling ↑ SV
- Aortic pressure ↑ 
- Action: ↑ SV + CO Response: ↑BP

- Forced expiration against closed glottis
- Superior and Inferior vena cava compressed → ↓ Venous returns → ↓ preload + ↑ afterload → ↓ CO via [[Control of Cardiac Output#Frank-Starling Law|Frank-Starling Law]]
