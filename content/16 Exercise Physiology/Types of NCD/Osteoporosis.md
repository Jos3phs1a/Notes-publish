progressive ==systemic bone disorder== characterised by **low bone mass** leading to bone fragility and consequent increased [[03a Fractures|fracture]] risk
often due to imbalance between bone formation and resorption

### risk factors
- ageing
- hormonal changes
- inadequate calcium & vitamin D intake
- sedentary lifestyle
- long term steroids

![[Pasted image 20250727173930.png|center]]

### Management
- Vitamin D & calcium supplements
- [[exercise effects on osteoporosis|exercise training]] 
	- improves bone mass
	- balance, vision, proprioception → fall risk
