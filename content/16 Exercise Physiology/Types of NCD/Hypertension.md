# Classification

## Primary
- 90-95% of cases, idiopathic
- SBP↑ as we age
	likely due to arterial stiffening, reduced collagen & elastin
- DBP plateau and decreases during 60s
	pulse pressure increases with age → ↑ mortality due to CVD risk

## Secondary
- 5-10% of cases
- Reversible
- complication of renal, endocrine, nervous system disorders
- defects in blood vessels during birth
- medication
