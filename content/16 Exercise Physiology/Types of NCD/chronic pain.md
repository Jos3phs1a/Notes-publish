## Side effects
- Increased sensitivity of nervous system → vulnerable to new threats and/or nociceptive stimuli
	results in increased pain during exercise
- Exercise associated with myofiber damage + substance release that could increase [[Nociceptors]] activity

## Should exercise still be conducted
understanding mechanisms of [[Endogenous Analgesia]] and chronic pain population 
in the end evidence suggest: 
- exercise is still more beneficial for person even if pain does not necessarily improve
- can overcome [[Disability threshold]], improve functional performance overall.

must mind patient's response to exercise

## Prescription & Monitoring
- should be sustainable, enjoyable 
- patient centered
- allow increased pain during and shortly after exercise but avoid continuously increasing pain intensity over time
- use time-contingent approach with appropriate baseline
- caution with eccentric loading + overly intense
