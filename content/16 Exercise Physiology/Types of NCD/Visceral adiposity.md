means accumulation of fat adipose tissue around abdominal area, ie fancy term for obesity

## effects
- ↑ pro-inflammatory adipokines
- ↓ protective adipokines
- associated with free fatty acids flux and insulin insensitivity, risk factor for [[Diabetes#Type 2|Type II Diabetes]]
- metabolic disturbance due to ectopic fat depots


## application to exercise
- intra-abdominal tissue most lipolytically active ie this type of fat breaks down and releases fat into bloodstream **faster and more readily** than other fat stores — making it more metabolically dynamic and potentially harmful in excess.
- fat source rarely contributes to oxidation process and is mostly broken down in [[Liver]]
- Most fatty acids that muscles oxdized come from subcutaneous adipose tissue

these factors result in long time to clear fat storage.
