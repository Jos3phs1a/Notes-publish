# Effect of exercise

- greater cholesterol efflux - HDL removes cholesterol from cells, transport to [[Liver]] for removal
- ↑ biological half-life of apolipoprotein AI(ApoA1) component of HDL complex, aids in removing cholesterol/fat from [[White blood cells]] within artery walls
- [[Endurance Exercise Prescription|Endurance training]] increase proportion of [[Type of muscle fibers|slow twitch fibers]], ↑ FA metabolism
- ↑ enzyme activity → removal TG-rich [[lipoproteins|lipoprotein]]
- ↓ presence of small and dense LDL particles and ↑ that of larger, more buoyant LDL particles
