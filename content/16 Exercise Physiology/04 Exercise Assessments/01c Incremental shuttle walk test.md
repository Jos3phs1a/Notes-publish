Incremental, progressive, symptom limited test to peak exercise capacity
designed to find patients max walk speed according to regular intervals

## Setup
- 2 cones, 10m apart, 0.5m from either end to avoid sudden change in direction
- #### Safety
Environment:
- set chairs along the route, if patient need rest
- ensure walking path clear from obstructions

Individual: 
- ensure patient in proper attire and footwear
- ensure no hearing deficits as test relies on audio
## Execution
- play standardized test audio 
- each 10m completed, tick 1 box on respective level
- Upon completion of every level, measure vitals
- When patient miss 2 cones in a row with clear margin or [[Contraindications for Exercise testing|Contraindication met]], terminate test

## Process results
- estimated walking speed at each level has been provided on sheet
- Take speed of corresponding level, regardless of no of laps completed
