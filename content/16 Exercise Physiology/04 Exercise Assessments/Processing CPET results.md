Using Measured results of [[Exercise data metrics#oxygen consumption|VO2]] or [[Exercise data metrics#Metrics of measuring Heart **Heart Rate** (HR) Heart Rate|HR]] we can 
1) predict a patient's current [[Exercise data metrics#VO$_2$ max|VO2max]] 
2) predict [[Exercise data metrics#Metrics of measuring Heart **Heart Rate** (HR) Heart Rate|HRmax]] 

via 2 methods

## Take face value
Typically taking measured value most accurate without any processing
due to patients meeting [[Contraindications for Exercise testing]] preventing further exertion so can take as limit.
