Assessments are to be stopped immediately upon troubling signs/symptoms or when requested

## Patient requests to terminate due to:
- intolerance
- palpitations
- cramps
- profound weakness/fatigue
- severe wheezing
- pains from comorbid conditions e.g. lower back pain

## Medical signs or symptoms
- Chest pain (angina/angina like symptoms)
- Shortness or breath
- Abnormal HR or ECG readings showing signs of ischemia
- ↓ SBP > 10mmHg during increasing work rate
- Sharp ↑ SBP > 250mmHg or DBP > 115mmHg
- signs of poor perfusion e.g. dizziness, fainting
- abnormal gait/loss of coordination
- mental confusion
- Failure of test equipment
