All Participants must not have the following conditions in order to be eligible for the assessments

## Absolute contraindications
- Acute myocardial infarction within 2 days
- ongoing unstable angina (within 1 month)
	- ==stable angina not absolute con==, test should be performed after patient has taken ==anti-angina medication with rescue nitrate available==
- uncontrolled cardiac arrhythmia with hemodynamic compromise
- active endocarditis
- symptomatic severe aortic stenosis
- decompensated heart failure
- acute pulmonary embolism, pulmonary infarction, deep vein thrombosis
- acute myocarditis or pericarditis
- acute aortic dissection
- physical disability that precludes safe and adequate testing
- severe pulmonary [[Hypertension]]

## Relative contraindications
- known obstructive left main coronary artery stenosis
- moderate to severe aortic stenosis with uncertain relationship to symptoms
- tachyarrhythmias with uncontrolled ventricular rates
- acquired advanced or complete heart block
- recent stroke or transient ischemia attack
- mental impairment with limited ability to cooperate
- resting [[Hypertension]] with ==SBP > 200mmHg== or  ==DBP < 110mmHg==
- uncorrected medical conditions e.g. significant anemia, important electrolyte imbalance, hyperthyroidism
- Pre exercise HR > ==125bpm==
- Resting SPO$_2$ < ==90%== (remeasure with BP machine or oxymeter)
- physical disability affecting safe performance e.g. unstable gait, balance issues



## TLDR
- Any cardiac related disease
- Any disease related to abnormal hemodynamics
- Anything that may deem patient to be unable to communicate well
