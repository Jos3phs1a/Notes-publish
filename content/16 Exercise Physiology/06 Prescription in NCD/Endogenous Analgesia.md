Pain reduction mechanisms within the body
mainly through 3 methods

- [[Physiological Effects of Electrotherapy#Endogenous Opioids|Endogenous opioids]] 
- Pain inhibition pathways
- [[Physiological Effects of Electrotherapy#Gate control theory|Pain gate theory]] 

These systems can be triggered using exercise
- Exercise induced hypoalgesia
	↓ pain post exercise
	For healthy population: training reduces perception of pain 
	For [[chronic pain]] population: magnitude and variation of effects due to variables dependent on pain condition & exercise intensity
- Exercise induced hyperalgesia
	↑ pain post exercise
