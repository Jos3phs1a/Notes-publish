# Effects

[[Endurance Exercise Prescription|Endurance training]] at moderate intensity 3-7 METs has shown benefits in prevention and mitigation of development of [[Diabetes#Type 2|Type II Diabetes]]
[[Dynamic resistance exercise]] also can help but for effect to be similar to endurance training, large muscle groups must be targeted frequently as METs only up to 4.5 
# Mechanism

## Enhance Insulin signaling and GLUT 4 expression
- repeated muscle contractions increase proteins responsible for signaling activity, found to be decreased in [[Diabetes#Type 2|Type II Diabetes]] and sedentary population
- Mediate [[GLUT]] translocation to membrane enhance skeletal muscle uptake and transport of glucose

## Activate AMPK
adenosine monophosphate activated protein kinase(AMPK): fuel sensing enzyme in mammalian cells activated by exercise by sensing changes in AMP/ATP ratio as a result of substrate oxidation 

plays a role in several processes within the cell to **restore energy homeostasis**.
stimulates usage of energy stores for oxidation

## Protect Pancreatic B cells
consumption of glucose and lipids reduce hyperglycemia and [[hyperlipidemia]] 
reduce pancreatic inflammation and oxidative stress

## Increase mitochondrial biogenesis
increase expression of [[transcriptional coactivators]] to make more mitochondria

## Enhance lipid metabolism
More lipids targeted for oxidation, reducing accumulation
also reduce peripheral insulin resistance
