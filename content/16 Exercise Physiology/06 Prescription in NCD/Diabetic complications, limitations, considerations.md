### Peripheral vascular disease
- evaluate based on signs and symptoms
- prevents continuous loading/strain during cardiovascular training

### Retinopathy
- ↑BP in eyes, affect vision 
- active proliferative retinopathy avoid strenuous exercise due to [[03 Response to resistance exercise#CVS|BP changes during resistance training]] 


### Nephropathy
- disease or damage affecting the [[00 kidney Anatomy|kidneys]] 
- avoid activities that cause systolic blood pressure to rise > 180mmHg (cases may differ)


### Neuropathy
#### Peripheral
- loss of protective sensation esp in feet
- require daily inspection & proper footwear to avoid complications e.g. ulcers, fractures
#### Autonomic
- limit individual's physical activity capacity, increase risk of CVD
- slow HR recovery ↑ mortality risk


### Medication
consider effects of medication and how it may impact exercise, some require intervention whereas others mostly safe to continue
