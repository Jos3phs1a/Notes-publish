## metabolic inflexibility
body poor ability to:
- oxidize fat despite increased availability
- switch between fat and carbohydrates as primary fuel substrates

## mitochondrial dysfunction
- Reduced expression of [[transcriptional coactivators]] PGC-1α and PGC-1β that activate transcription factors affecting metabolic process
- decreased type 2a muscle fibers and overall oxidative capacity due to decrease in key enzyme(citrate synthase)
- decreased mitochondria amount
