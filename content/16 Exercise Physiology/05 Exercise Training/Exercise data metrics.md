## oxygen consumption
### VO$_2$ max
 **maximum amount of oxygen** an individual can utilize during intense or maximal exercise
==can either be measured or predicted==
### VO$_2$ Reserve
Oxygen consumption difference between max and resting ie accounts for baseline fitness level at rest
==main use is for [[exercise training]] estimation==
$$\%VO_2=\%(VO_{2max}-VO_{2rest})+VO_{2rest}$$

### Formulas
#### Walking
$$VO_2=[0.1*Speed(m/min)] + (1.8*speed*gradient(\%))+3.5$$
#### Running
$$VO_2=[0.2*Speed(m/min)+[0.9*speed*gradient]+3.5 $$
#### Cycle Ergometry
$$VO_2=1.8*\frac{workrate(kgm/min)}{Weight(kg)}+7$$
$$VO_2=10.8*\frac{Power(W)}{Weight(kg)}+7$$
### Note
- ==1 Watt = 6kgm/min== , 1.8x6 = 10.8
- ==Actual VO$_2$ max and predicted== via ACSM equation differ due to many factors like age, gender, fitness etc.
## Metabolic Equivalents (METS)
unit of measurement that represents the amount of oxygen the body uses during activity
At rest, 1 MET = 3.5 ml/kg/min VO$_2$ ==(will differ based on individual but is good estimate)==
