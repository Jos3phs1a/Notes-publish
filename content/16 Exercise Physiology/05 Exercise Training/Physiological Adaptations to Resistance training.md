# metabolic/muscle adaptations

improved:
- motor unit recruitment
- neuromuscular junctions - motor nerves controlling contractions affect muscle length shortening
- contractile proteins
- muscle CSA ([[hypertrophy]])
- fast-twitch fibers + slow-twitch fibers
- anaerobic energy stores (PCr, ATP)

# CVS adaptations 

- large unchanged VO$_{2max}$ submaximal VO2
- possible increase in left vent and septal thickness, may be related to body mass

# Neural Adaptations

- ↑ CNS activation
- ↑ Efficiency in motor neuron firing pattern and rates
- ↓ inhibitory reflexes and [[golgi tendon organs]], ensure precision of tone and control

# Others

- ↑ connective tissue for tendons and ligaments
- ↑ bone density
- ↓ perceived soreness post exercise session

## Effects on varying demographics
strength and conditioning affects
- performance athletes 
	lower injury risk
	increased performance

- Elderly
	reduced frailty via ↑ functional capacity, better gait/stair climbing ability + lower fall risk

- groups with pathology resulting in muscle loss e.g. sarcopenia, cachexia, diabetes
	↑ muscle strength, mass, physical performance
	decreases severe sarcopenia
