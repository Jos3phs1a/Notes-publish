
## Structure
- mostly type 1 collagen fiber, elastin
- arranged in parallel along longitudinal axis of ligament
- Follow [[elasticity model]]


## Function
- transmit forces between bones
- stabilize synovial monosyonival joints by guiding motion + prevent abnormal motion
- joint proprioception 
- engage protective response
- when muscles cannot withstand load, ligaments begin to stretch, overstretching leads to [[04 PTY/103 Clinical Applications/Pathophysiology/Soft tissue injuries/ligamentous injuries|ligamentous injuries]]
