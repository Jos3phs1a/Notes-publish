### Types of Movement
most movements occur in an infinite number of vertical and horizontal planes

parallel to the cardinal planes.

- Characteristics:
- Multi-joint
- Multi-muscle
- Multiplanar
- Occurs in a functional position (needed for activities of daily living)
- Incorporates balance
- Requires core stability

7 basic movements:

1. squat
2. lunge
3. Push
4. Pull
5. Bend
6. Twist
7. Gait

**Movements of the body**

Joint motion that occurs only in one plane is designated as one degree of freedom; in two planes, two degrees of freedom; and in three planes, three degrees of freedom

TLDR [[types of joints|type of joint]] determines the degree of movement

### Axes of Rotation

- **Mediolateral Axis**
	Runs medial/lateral (left right)
	lies on frontal plane
- **Anteroposterior Axis**
	Runs anterior/posterior (front back)
	same orientation as sagittal plane

- **Supra-inferior(SI) Axis**
	Runs superior/ inferior (up & down)

- **Diagonal Oblique Axis**
	right angle to diagonal plane
