### Muscle Training

**Strengthening:**

increase muscle maximal force generation and muscle endurance

mostly loaded exercises, with resistance

PROCESS acronym:

Progression, Regularity, Overload, Creativity, Enjoyment, Specificity, Supervision

**Re-education:**

Focus on engaging neuromuscular system to remember how it works/act before injury or surgery

Aim to reestablish neuromuscular control or promote the ability of muscle (group) to contract.
