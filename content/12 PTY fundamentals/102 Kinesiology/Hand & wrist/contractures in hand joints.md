## Flexion contractures 
[[Contractures]] occurs in [[00 Hand & Wrist#Hand Joints|Finger Joints]] 
- During flexion, [[Structure of MCP joints#Palmar plates|Palmar Plates]] slides proximal to condyles
- becomes adherent to filmy soft tissue between tendon sheath and periosteum
- maintaining this positioning results in contracture

## Extension contractures
occurs in [[00 Hand & Wrist#Metacarpophalangeal(MCP) Joints|MCP joints]] 
- [[Structure of MCP joints#Collateral ligaments|Collateral ligaments]] slacken during extension
- prolonged extension leads to shortening → contracture formed
