



## PIP joint

--- start-multi-column: ID_qqrn
```column-settings
Number of Columns: 2
Largest Column: standard
```


#### Articular surfaces
- ==Convex Head of Proximal Phalanx== articulates with ==Concave base of Middle Phalanx==
- Tongue- in groove articulation preventing axial rotation, keep movement to 1 plane

#### Ligaments
similar to [[Structure of MCP joints]] in terms of ligamentous structures

PIP joint additional of **Check-rein ligaments** ^251418
- reinforce proximal attachments of the palmar plate 
- limiting hyperextension of the joint.

## DIP joint
#### Articular Surfaces
- ==Convex Head of Middle phalanx== articulate with ==Concave base of distal phalanx==
#### Ligaments

similar to PIP joint without [[Structure of IP joints#^251418|Check-rein ligaments]]
