# overview
Structure of hip is built for stability unlike the [[00 Shoulder]] which is built for mobility
thus, its design does not contribute heavily hip movement

Each side of the pelvis aka [[Innominate]], is union of the: **ilium, pubis & ischium** → connections form a complete osteoligamentous
ring, known as the **pelvis**

### Functions
- transmit large forces between trunk & ground
	- hip abductors on WB leg must withstand ~85% body weight every step if not pelvis will drop on one side
- Elevate and lower the body
- Locomote
- Serve as attachments for various muscles of the lower limb and trunk
- support organs of bower, bladder, reproductive function
