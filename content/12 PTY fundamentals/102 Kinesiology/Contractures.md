Adaptive shortening of muscle-tendon unit + soft tissue crossing or around joint
	Loss of elastin, reducing ROM



| Types                                    | Description                                                                                                                  | Response to treatment                                     |
| :--------------------------------------- | ---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- | --------------------------------------------------------- |
| Myostatic                                | Adaptive shortening + Loss ROM less sarcomeres but no change in length, no specific pathology                                | Stretching exercise                                       |
| Pseudomyostatic                          | Spasticity/rigidity due to neurological condition, pain, muscles in sustain/tetanus contraction                              | Address source of pain or hypertonicity (solute >water)   |
| Athrogenic & periarticular(around joint) | Tissue adhesion, synovial proliferation, joint effusion, impaired cartilage/bony formation; joint capsule lose extensibility | May require joint mobilization before stretching exercise |
| Fibrotic                                 | Fibrous change e.g. fibrosis                                                                                                 | Hard to rehab, Require Surgery                            |
