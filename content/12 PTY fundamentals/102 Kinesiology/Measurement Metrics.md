Crucial to consider for both [[Different Muscle Tests]] 
### Validity

Aka accuracy

### Reliability
Repeatability/consistency
To improve:
- Proper positioning
- Adequate stabilization
- Patient's ability to maintain position
- Consistent timing, pressure, position
- Avoid preconceived impressions  of test outcomes
- Avoid pain

### Responsiveness
Sensitivity to change
- Minimal detectable change - diff in measurements required to reflect true change e.g. spot improvements
- Minimal clinically important difference - smallest diff in measurements crucial to patient
