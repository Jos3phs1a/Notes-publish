# Movement








### movement of scapula region

Scapula joint movements happen in tandem
Due to natural anatomy ie muscle attachment, contours of the ribs
Scapulothoracic movements result as a combination of SC and AC movements

| Scapulothoracic                 | Sternoclavicular           | Acromioclavicular              |
| ------------------------------- | -------------------------- | ------------------------------ |
| Elevation/<br>Depression        | Elevation/<br>Depression   | Anterior/<br>Posterior tilting |
| Protraction/<br>Retraction      | Protraction/<br>Retraction | Internal/<br>External Rotation |
| Upward/<br>Downward<br>Rotation | Elevation/<br>Depression   | Upward/<br>Downward Rotation   |
