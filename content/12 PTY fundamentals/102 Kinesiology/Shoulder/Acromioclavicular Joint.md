
## Structure
### **Articular Surface**
- Between Clavicle & Acromion
- Plane Synovial joint allowing 3 rotational + 3 translational degrees of freedom

### Ligaments
1. Superior + Inferior acromioclavicular joint ligaments
2. Coracoclavicular ligament: 
	conoid portion: vertical, limit excess movement distal end of clavicle
	trapezoid portion: vertical/lateral, resist inferior medial force 
3. Coracoacromial ligament: restrains head of humerus
4. Accessory ligaments

## Movements
contributes most to shoulder movement at **End range of motion**
- ER/IR - 30°
- Ant/Post tilt - 30-40°
- Upward/Downward rotation - 30° & 17° respectively
