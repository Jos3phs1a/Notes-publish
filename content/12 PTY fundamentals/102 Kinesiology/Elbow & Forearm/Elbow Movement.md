### Radius

--- start-multi-column: ID_hlor
```column-settings
Number of Columns: 2
Largest Column: standard
```

Concave fovea(radius) rolls & slides on convex capitulum

does not provide as much stability as ulna due to smaller size

but provides 50% resistance
against lateral forces
via
1. shape of angulation
2. depth of fovea
3. compression forces produced
by muscles crossing elbow

## Supination/Pronation

--- start-multi-column: ID_yyo5
```column-settings
Number of Columns: 2
Largest Column: standard
```

Proximal: 
Ulna stationary
radial head **rotates** within fibro-osseous ring

Distal: 
Ulna stationary
==concave ulnar notch of radius slides== on ulnar head
articular disc sweeps across ulnar head

for **pronation**: 
1. dorsal capsular ligament at distal RU joint elongates
due to tension
2. palmar capsular ligament slackens to ==70% original length==
