- crescent shape
- fibrocartilage articular surfaces within knee joint
- 2 on each knee, medial & lateral
- lateral more mobile 
- anchored to intercondylar region of tibia via **ant & post horns** (also fibrocartilage)
- **coronary ligaments** attach tibia to capsule → loose for movement
- **Transverse ligament** link both menisci

### Blood supply
peripheral 1/3 from popliteal artery
inner 1/3 from synovial fluid
# Function

- Reduce compressive stress across tibiofemoral joint
- stabilize joint during motion
- lubricate articular cartilage
- provide proprioception
- guide knee arthrokinematics
- reduce pressure on articular cartilage by tripling joint contact area
- attachment site for other structures
	- quadriceps
	- semimembranosus
	- popliteus to lateral meniscus


Relevant to [[Meniscal Injury]]
