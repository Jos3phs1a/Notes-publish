# Open Chain movement
Associated with [[Knee Osteokinematics#tibia-on-femur movement|Tibia-on-femur movement]] 



![[Pasted image 20250329183757.png|center|800]]

Most torque generated at C (full extension) 
- v hard to maintain
- high strain on [[Knee ligaments#^782d0c|ACL]] due to ==tibial anterior translation== 


# Closed Chain movement

Associated with [[Knee Osteokinematics#femur-on-tibia movement|Femur-on-tibia movement]]

![[Pasted image 20250329184220.png|center]]

Opposite to open chain, 
- highest torque is during D (Full Knee Flexion)
