- between [[Patella]] and trochlear groove of femur

## Movement
### Tibial-on-femoral knee flexion
patella slides on fixed trochlear groove of femur
patella pulled in direction of tibia because of patellar tendon
### Femoral-on-Tibia knee flexion
trochlear groove slides relative to fixed patella

# Bowstringing effect

Attachment of muscles and ligaments on patella are such that a lateral pull is created, 
if not offset→ patella slides laterally causing:
- reduce contact area of joint
- ↑ joint pressure and stress
- ↑ chance of dislocation

Factors contributing to bowstringing
