# Structure

latin for small plate

### Anterior Surface
Apex = patellar tendon attaches proximally

### Posterior Surface
covered by articular cartilage up to ==7.75mm thick==
**vertical ridge** runs through center longitudinally creating lateral and medial facets for articulation with patellofemoral joint

![[Pasted image 20250328010241.png|InR]] gray area on anterior surface = attachment of quad muscles
# Function


- centralize force of quad muscles to one point 
- allow smooth gliding of quad muscles and tendon to reduce compression and friction during knee flexion
- ↑ stability of knee
- protect femur from trauma
- Increase torque generated by quad muscles during knee extn via ↑ moment arm
