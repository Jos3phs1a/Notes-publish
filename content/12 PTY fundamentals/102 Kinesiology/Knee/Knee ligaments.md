# 4 Tibiofemoral joint ligaments

| Ligament                                | Proximal attachment                                                                 | Distal attachment                                                                                                                      | Function                                                                                                        | Features                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                             |
| --------------------------------------- | ----------------------------------------------------------------------------------- | -------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- | --------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- | ------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------ |
| **Medial Collateral ligament (MCL)**    | Medial epicondyle of femur                                                          | Posterior section → medial epicondyle of femur<br><br>Anterior section → middle superior aspect of medial tibia distal to pes anserine | Resist [[Alignment of Knee#^fac0b5\|valgus force]] in frontal plane                                             | **Superficial portion**: 10cm across medial epicondyle to medial=proximal tibia<br><br>**Deep portion**: shorter oblique fibres, distal and posterior to superficial fibers<br>attach to [[Knee Joint capsules#^8eafaa\|posterior-medial capsule, medial meniscus, semimembranosus]] |
| **Lateral Collateral Ligament (LCL)**   | Lateral epicondyle of femur                                                         | Fibular Head lateral aspect<br>bicep femoris tendon                                                                                    | Resist [[Alignment of Knee#^94b68d\|varus force]] in frontal plane                                              | short, cordlike, vertical, blends with bicep femoris tendon, no attachment to lateral meniscus<br><br>popliteus tendon runs between lateral meniscus & LCL                                                                                                                           |
| **Anterior Collateral Ligament (ACL)**  | Anterior inter-condylar tibial fossa, behind anterior attachment of medial meniscus | Postero-medial aspect of lateral femoral condyle                                                                                       | prevent anterior displacement of tibia on femur during knee extension<br>secondary restraint to tibial rotation | - intracapsular ligaments<br>- poor blood supply → reduced healing <br>-able to withstand up to 1800N<br>- multi planar dynamic stability<br>- proprioception capabilities                                                                                                           |
| **Posterior Collateral Ligament (PCL)** | Posterior tibial spine                                                              | anterolateral surface of medial femoral condyle                                                                                        | prevent posterior displacement of tibia on femur during knee flexion                                            | ^                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                    |
