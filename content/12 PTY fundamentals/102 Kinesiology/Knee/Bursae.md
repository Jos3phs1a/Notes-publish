# structure

- knee has 14 bursae
- form at inter-tissue junctions that encounter friction
- either extensions of synovial membrane or external capsules
- some contain fat pads e.g. suprapatellar and deep infrapatellar bursae

# Function

- reduce friction
- absorb shock
