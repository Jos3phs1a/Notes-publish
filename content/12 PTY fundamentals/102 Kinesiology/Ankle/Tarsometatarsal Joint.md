aka Lisfranc joint complex

articulation between the tarsus in [[00 Foot & Ankle#Midfoot|midfoot]] and metatarsal bases
- 1st, 2nd, 3rd metatarsals articulate with respective cuneiforms
- 4th and 5th metatarsals with cuboid.

Numerous dorsal and plantar ligaments + interosseous ligament support the joints
[[Ankle ligaments#Lisfranc ligament|Lisfranc ligament]] most important in stability
