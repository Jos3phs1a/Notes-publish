
# Deltoid ligament

- reinforce medial side of ankle
- limit extremes of eversion across [[Talocrural Joint]],  [[Subtalar Joint]],  talonavicular joint

4 Fibers:
1) tibionavicular fibers
2) Tibiospring fibers
3) Tibiocalcaneal fibers
4) Tibiotalar fibers


# Spring ligament

- thick, wide band spanning gap between ==sustentaculum talus and calcaneus== 
- maintains medial longitudinal arch of foot and contributes to [[Medial longitudinal arch]]

**Superficial components:** 
1) tibiocalcaneal
2) tibionavicular
3) posterior superficial tibiotalar
4) tibiospring

**Deep components:**
1) Anterior tibiotalar ligament
2) posterior deep tibiotalar ligament

# Lateral Collateral ligaments

### Anterior talofibular ligament
- limits inversion, adduction, plantarflexion at [[Talocrural Joint]] 
- most frequently injured 

### Posterior talofibular ligament
- limits abduction, inversion, dorsiflexion at [[Talocrural Joint]]

### Calcaneofibular ligament
- limit inversion, dorsiflexion across [[Talocrural Joint]] & [[Subtalar Joint]]

# Lisfranc ligament



![[Pasted image 20250712162258.png]]

most important ligament in [[Tarsometatarsal Joint]] complex

proximal attachments: plantar aspect of medial cuneiform
distal attachments: base of 2nd Metatarsal

3 bands:
- dorsal ligament: weakest
- interosseous ligament ("Lisfranc ligament proper")
- plantar ligament: sends bundles to the second and third metatarsal bases (variable)
