# Structure

Known as midtarsal or Chopart's joints
have strong functional relationship with [[Subtalar Joint]]

2 Joints function together to **control pronation/supination** of foot → contours of foot on uneven surfaces
## Talonavicular Joint
- medial component of transverse tarsal joint
- resembles [[types of joints#^071d00|Ball-and-socket]] joint type articulation
- articulation between convex **head of talus** and, concave **proximal navicular bone**
- Reinforced by [[Ankle ligaments#Spring ligament]]  

## Calcaneocuboid Joint
- lateral component of transverse tarsal joint
- articulation between anterior surface of calcaneus and proximal surface of cuboid
- each articular surface has concave & convex curvature
- ==less motion== than [[#Talonavicular Joint]], esp in frontal & horizontal planes, providing ==stability for lateral column of foot==
