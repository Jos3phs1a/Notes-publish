# Structure
2 to 2.5 mm thick, series of longitudinal & transverse bands of collagen-rich tissue
cover sole and sides superficially and deep resist up to 800N ==before permanent elongation==

Attachments
Proximal: posterior medial process of calcaneal tuberosity 
Distal:  divides into five processes at metatarsal heads, one for each of the toes

contributes heavily to [[arches of the foot]] and in functional activity during gait observed through [[Medial longitudinal arch]]
