Recall innervation of leg muscles ADLS
anterior = deep fibular nerve
supply FEET

lateral = superficial fibular nerve
supply fibular muscles

--- column-break ---

![[Pasted image 20250330192522.png]]

--- end-multi-column


### Injury to deep branch
paralysis of dorsiflexors causing **drop foot/pes equinus**
plantar flexion contracture

### Injury to superficial branch
paralysis of fibularis muscles evertors
**pes varus**

### Injury to common nerve
paralysis to all muscles
**pes equinovarus**


# Tibial nerve

supplies posterior muscles
![[Pasted image 20250330192711.png]]
### proximal nerve injury
paralysis of all plantarflexor and supinator 
**pes calcaneovalgus**

### Middle portion injury
paralysis of supinator muscles
**pes valgus**

### medial & lateral plantar nerve injury
paralysis of intrinsic muscles of foot
**claw toes**
