# Structure

- Resides under talus
- Articulations between posterior, middle, anterior facets(PF, MF, AF) of **calcaneus & talus**
- ==70% of surface area== of joint occupied by posterior articulation between **concave PF of talus** and **convex PF of calcaneus**

## ligaments
- Calcaneofibular ligament → inversion
- deltoid ligament → eversion
- interosseous cervical → bind talus with calcaneus, limits extremes of al motion esp ==inversion== 


# Osteokinematics

Primary allow pronation & supination, occurs in an oblique plane, 
Pronation = eversion + abduction 
Supination = inversion + adduction 

*calcaneus moves slight dorsiflexion and plantarflexion relative to talus, but small motion and ignored clinically*
