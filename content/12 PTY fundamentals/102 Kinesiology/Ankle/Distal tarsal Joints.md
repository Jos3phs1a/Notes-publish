# Tarsometatarsal Joints

- articulation between distal cuneiforms or cuboid & metatarsals

# Metatarsophalangeal Joints

- articulation between convex head of distal metatarsals and shallow concave proximal phalanx 

# Interphalangeal Joints

### PIP
articulation between convex proximal phalanx and concave middle phalanx

### DIP
articulation between convex middle phalanx and concave distal phalanx

1st ray only one IP joint
