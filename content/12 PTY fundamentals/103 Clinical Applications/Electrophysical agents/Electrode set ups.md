# Prechecks

- ensure electrode gel/adhesive ==even + not dried== → risk of uneven current + **skin burns**
	- gel reduces resistance between electrode and skin
- ensure no ==allergic reactions== to adhesive
- size of electrodes suitable for targeting treatment area
	- bigger muscle → bigger pad
	- affects [[charge density]]

# Placement

--- start-multi-column: ID_j92h
```column-settings
Number of Columns: 2
Largest Column: standard
```

1) smooth on skin, no wrinkles/gaps
	- affects [[charge density]] 
2) not placed on bony prominences 
	- ↑ resistance + poor adhesion → risk of skin burns
3) Distance between electrodes
	- affects path and depth of current, ==further gap → deeper effect==

- over or on top of painful area
- around painful area
- along dermatome associated with painful area
- acupuncture/trigger points

# Arrangements

### Monopolar

--- start-multi-column: ID_wtvj
```column-settings
Number of Columns: 2
Largest Column: standard
```

- 1 electrode over treatment area, other electrode placed where effect not intended (grounding)
- lead wires from current source can be split for 2 or more electrodes to use on treatment area
- used for [[iontophoresis]] and wound healing via [[Parameters of Electrical Stimulation#Direct|DC]] & [[Parameters of Electrical Stimulation#Monophasic|Mono pulsed current]]

--- start-multi-column: ID_wmb5
