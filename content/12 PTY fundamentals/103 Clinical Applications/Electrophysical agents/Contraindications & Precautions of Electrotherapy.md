TLDR; look out for
- pacemakers
- abnormal cardiac activity/conditions
- abnormal hemodynamic
- pregnant woman abdomen → may induce contractions
- sensory impairments
- cognition/communication impairments
- Open wounds
- Chest or intercostal muscles
- bone disorders e.g. fractures, osteoporosis
