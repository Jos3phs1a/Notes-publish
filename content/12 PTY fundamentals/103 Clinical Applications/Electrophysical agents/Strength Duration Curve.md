
relationship between duration and amplitude of electrical current required to stimulate a type of nerve

### Implications
Choosing the right amplitude and duration helps:

- Minimize discomfort
    
- Target the correct nerve type (sensory vs motor vs Denervated)
    
- Avoid stimulating pain fibers unnecessarily

-  Shorter durations are generally **more comfortable** and safer
    
- Longer durations may be used in [[NMES effects#on Denervated Muscles|Denervated muscles]] or [[Function of Electrical Stimulation#^6226a9|pain management]], but with caution
