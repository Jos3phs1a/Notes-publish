## [[NMES effects|Muscle Strengthening & Reeducation]]
- Place 1 electrode over [[motor point]] 
- Place other electrode on intended muscle such that current ==travels parallel to direction of muscle fibers==
- Tetanus contraction observed + instruct patient to move along with contraction and hold isometrically
## [[NMES effects#Reduce Spasm/ edema|Edema Control]]
- patient elevated 
- electrodes surround swollen area

## [[Parameters of Electrical Stimulation#On time and off time|On & off times]]
- give muscles time to contract and relax during treatment
- + better gains

==Ideal ratio = 1:5== e.g. 6-10s on → 50-120s off
When patients progress, ratio can be lowered

[[#NMES effects Reduce Spasm/ edema Edema Control|Edema Control]] and spasms
- ratio = 1:1 
- On/off times = 2-5s

Note: [[Parameters of Electrical Stimulation#Ramp-up and ramp down time|Ramp times]] have to be factored for on off durations
