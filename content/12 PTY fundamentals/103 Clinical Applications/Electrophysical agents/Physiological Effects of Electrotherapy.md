# Ionic Effect and tissue healing

## Mechanism
[[Parameters of Electrical Stimulation#Direct|DC]],[[Parameters of Electrical Stimulation#Monophasic|Monophasic pulsed current]], [[Parameters of Electrical Stimulation#Biphasic|Unbalanced Biphasic]](rare)  leave +ve -ve charges on tissues, effect useful in chronic wounds,
### tissue healing 
- [[Galvanotaxis]] facilitating ==improved circulation== → flow of cells like [[White blood cells]] and [[Function of platelets in Hemostasis#^2aa5a6|fibroblasts]] to injured area to activate and begin healing process 
- reduce formation of [[edema]] caused by inflammation or by lack of motion. 

****should not be used** to treat edema suspected to have a ==systemic cause== because may drive fluid from extremities into central circulation, further ==overwhelming the failing organ system.==* ^596099
