### Pulsed 
#### Biphasic

--- start-multi-column: ID_eico
```column-settings
Number of Columns: 2
Largest Column: standard
```

- Current with 2 polarities
- +ve and =ve phases may be ==consecutive== or ==separated by short interval== (**interpulse**)
- can be symmetrical (balanced charges) or asymmetrical(waveform different charges ==either balanced or unbalanced==)

![[Pasted image 20250616173156.png|center]]

--- end-multi-column
#### Monophasic

--- start-multi-column: ID_el0t
```column-settings
Number of Columns: 2
Largest Column: standard
```

- one direction, asymmetrical, charge unbalanced
- constant polarity diff between electrodes
- presence of interpulse interval

**Low volt**
- rectangular pulses e.g. microcurrent

**High volt**
- high voltage pulsed current
- spike phase of equal amplitude and polarity
- for wound healing + [[edema]]

# Phase vs Pulse duration/width


--- start-multi-column: ID_5qze
```column-settings
Number of Columns: 2
Largest Column: standard
```

Phase duration:
how long a phase lasts

Pulse duration:
time from beginning of first phase to end of last phase of pulse

both measured in microseconds (µs)
