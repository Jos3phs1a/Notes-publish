## Guidelines

1. What mechanism of pain modulation is most suitable here?
2. What sensation will be most tolerable and effective for this patient?
3. Is this patient new to TENS, or have experienced TENS before?
4. Do I aim to provide immediate relief or sustained analgesia?

High rate → most comfortable
