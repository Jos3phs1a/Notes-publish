
## Treatment surface temperatures
Difference between surface temperatures
higher difference = ↑ rate of cooling

## Time of exposure
duration tissue is exposed to cold

## Thermal conductivity
Different tissues transfer heat at different rates
[[temperature regulation#^09d13c|adipose tissue]] conduct heat slower than muscle tissue due to layer of fat 

## Area of tissue affected
↑ area exposed = ↑ rate of transfer

## Tissue depth
superficial tissue experience change within 1 min
deeper tissues take longer

## Form of cold therapy
different methods provide varying results and degrees of cooling, must cater properly to patient/client

E.g. [[Methods of energy transfer#Ice Packs|Ice packs]] provide larger magnitude change compared to [[Methods of energy transfer#Ice bath|Ice baths]] or gel packs

## Post treatment activity
Muscular exertion ↑ rate of rewarming
