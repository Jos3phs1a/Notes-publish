High threshold sensory neuron that transduces and encodes noxious stimuli
aka potential tissue damage
are polymodal and detect different stimuli:
- mechanical
- thermal
- chemical

subdivided into 2 major fibers

## A-delta fibers
**myelinated** and have **small receptive fields** responsible for the initial perception of pain
typically responsible for **sharp, well-localized, fast pain**

## C fibers
**unmyelinated** and have **large receptive fields**, which allow them to relay pain intensity
responsible for **dull, aching, burning, or throbbing pain** after sharp initial pain
