## overview
- Applies concept of wave interference, 2 [[Parameters of Electrical Stimulation#Alternating|ACs]] of medium frequencies cross each other → new waveform created
- rationale behind creation: 
	apply high frequency current that passes through skin easily 
	requires less electrical energy 
	less painful

ie resulting waves is summation of highs + subtraction of lows

resulting wave measured in **beats** ==(envelope of pulses)==
amplitude is the ==net diff between the frequencies== of original wave
