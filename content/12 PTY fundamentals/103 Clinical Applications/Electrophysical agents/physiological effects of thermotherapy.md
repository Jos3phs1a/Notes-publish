## Vasodilation
Increase in temperature activates multistep progress that results in increased blood flow around treatment area 
Effective differs based on temperature within [[therapeutic window]] 

## Neuromuscular 

### Nerve conduction velocity
- Increased temperature generally increase firing rate of nerves by 2m/s every 1°C
- **Demyelinated nerves**: cause **conduction blocks** due ↓ duration of Na$^+$ channels opening at [[Myelinated and Demyelinated Nerves|Nodes of Ranvier]]  → nerves unable to depolarize and send signals
- Precaution during applications on *demyelinating conditions e.g. carpal tunnel syndrome. multiple sclerosis*  

### Nerve firing rate (frequency)
1) Increased temperatures alter firing rates via:
- ↓ type II muscle spindle(afferent) + gamma efferents → ↓ afferent signals to spinal cord → ↓ stretch reflex → muscle relax + ↓ spasms
- ↑ type Ib fibers from [[golgi tendon organs]](GTO) → inhibitory signals to alpha motor neurons → relaxation

[Reference](Fischer M, Schafer SS: Temperature effects on the discharge frequency of primary and secondary endings of isolated cat muscle spindles recorded under a ramp-and-hold stretch. Brain Res 840:1–15, 1999.)
### Increase pain threshold
**Proposed Mechanism**
1) **Direct & Immediate**
	- ↑ activity of cutaneous thermoreceptors 
	- Gating effect of pain sensation at spinal cord level ie pain signals that reach the spinal cord are dampened
2) **Indirect and Prolonged**
	- decreased ischemia via ↑ Blood flow
	- ↓ muscle spasm, that compress blood vessels
	

### Changes in muscle strength
- Initially reduced for 30 mins after application
- Restores fully after 2 hours 
- growing evidence of [[Delayed increase in strength using heat]] 
#### Causes
- ↓ firing rate of alpha motor neurons,  due to firing rate change of type II muscle spindle efferent, gamma efferents, type 1b fibers 
- motor neurons less active, muscles unable to generate optimal tension
### Connective tissue
With added stretching along with modality, can alter [[Mechanics of Soft Tissue#Property of Biological Tissue|viscoelastic properties]] 6of tissue
- Longer lasting elongation after completion of stretch
- Effectiveness dependent on temp elevation of treatment area and stretch

### Metabolic 
Increase temperature causes
- ↑ cell activity and metabolic rate
- ↑ O$_2$ uptake by tissues 
	-due to O$_2$ dissociation curve shifting right, O$_2$ used up more readily
