when not to use cryotherapy on patients

#### persons with cognition or communication problems
safety hazard
#### cold urticaria
cold allergy/hypersensitivity 

#### Raynaud's disease
pale skin + redness at digits/nose

#### Cryoglobulinemia
cooling causes serum protein to cause blood clots

#### hemoglobinemia
autoimmune disease where proteins attach to RBC during cold causing rupture

#### areas of impaired circulation
cryotherapy will only exacerbate the poor circulation

#### persons with active deep vein thrombosis or thrombophlebitis 

#### near chronic wounds
worsens healing process 

#### over regenerating nerves
allodynia, light stimulus causing pain

#### tissues affected by tuberculosis 
vasoconstriction ↓ $O_2$ + WBC circulation, impairing immune system
#### hemorrhaging tissue in person with untreated hemorrhagic disorders

#### anterior neck and carotid sinus
leading to sudden ↓ HR & BP
