## Ice Packs & Massages
ice applied over small area, 
move around in strokes or circles for massage
applied ==10-15 mins==
patient feels:
	- cold
	- burning (lasts 1-2mins)
	- aching (lasts 1-2mins)
	- numbness
avoid prolonged exposure/too large area leading to [[Contraindications of cryotherapy]] 
## Immersions
Ice baths
Cold whirlpools
good for distal extremities where surfaces are more awkward to apply on

## Vapocoolant sprays 
e.g. ethyl chloride
superficial, cooling agent for reduction of muscle guarding and release of myofascial trigger points


## Cryocompression unit

- Manual circulation of cold water through cuff applied over extremity
- compression caused by pressure from fluids filling the cuff 
- typically used after surgery
