# Absorption

![[Pasted image 20250524124621.png]]

Generally, Tissues with higher collagen + lower water more efficient in absorption

### Relationship with Intensity
Absorption of mechanical energy follows exponential pattern
drastically decrease at start as you go deeper into the tissue(regular intervals)
More energy is absorbed in the superficial tissues than in deep tissue
**Half-value depth**: depth at which 50% of ultrasound absorbed by tissue, varies based on frequency and tissue treated

# Reflection

Ultrasound is unable to penetrate [[Anatomy of Hard Tissue|Hard tissue]] due to density
majority of ultrasonic energy is reflected, 
- may bounce back into treatment area ↑ heating 
- cause [[periosteal pain]] if applied over subcutaneous bone
