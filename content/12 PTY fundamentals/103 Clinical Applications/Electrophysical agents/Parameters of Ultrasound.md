# Frequency
ultrasound frequencies typically range between 1 MHz to 3MHz 

1 MHz: deeper tissue 5 or more cm
3 MHz: superficial  0.8 to 3cm
# Mode
aka duration/duty cycle
Selected mode depends on desired healing effect and stage of treatment
Non-thermal: Acute inflammatory phase → ↓ [[#Duty cycle]]
thermal: later phase of healing → ↑ [[#Duty cycle]]
### Continuous
100% duty cycle
energy produced throughout whole duration selected
### Pulsed
Energy produced at ranged intervals
Less intense heating, ↓ thermal effects ↑ non-thermal effects 
#### Duty cycle
% of time ultrasound energy is produced from transducer

$$Duty \:Cycle=\frac{Pulse\:duration(on\:time)}{Period\:duration(on\,+\,off\:time)}$$

#### Pulse ratio
ratio of on time to off time
50% duty cycle = 1:1 
25% = 1:3
10% = 1:9
