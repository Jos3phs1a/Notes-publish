- 2 tubs of hot(38-44°C) and cold water(10-18°C)
- alternate immersions to control temperature
### method
- immerse treatment area in warm water for 3-10 mins followed by cold water for 1 -3 mins
- repeat 5-6 times for total treatment time of 25-30 mins

| Advantages                                                          | Disadvantages                                |
| ------------------------------------------------------------------- | -------------------------------------------- |
| promotes increased superficial blood flow                           | limb in dependent position, aggravates edema |
| provides good contact with treatment areas compared to other agents | some patients do not tolerate cold immersion |
| pain control without aggravating edema                              |                                              |
| allows for movement for increased circulatory effects               |                                              |
