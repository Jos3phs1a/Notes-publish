2 types of restrictions:

## capsular pattern
proportional limitation of **passive ROM**/pain often due to arthrogenic causes
certain movements ==are more restricted== than others
e.g.
- Capsular inflammation (e.g., adhesive capsulitis,[[osteoarthritis]])
- Synovial inflammation (e.g., synovitis)
- Joint effusion (e.g., trauma)
- Fibrosis or contracture of the capsule

![[Pasted image 20250807120031.png]]


## Non-capsular patterns

any pattern other than a [[#capsular pattern]], and may indicate the presence of either a derangement, a restriction of one part of the joint capsule, or an extra-articular lesion, that obstructs joint motion.****

**variable or irregular limitation of movement**, does **not** follow joint's capsular pattern 

**Extra-articular** or **specific internal** causes such as:
- [[Muscular injury]]
- [[04 PTY/103 Clinical Applications/Pathophysiology/Soft tissue injuries/ligamentous injuries|ligamentous injuries]]
- [[01 Pathology and aetiology|Tendinopathy]] 
- Loose body in joint
- Meniscal or labral injury
- Nerve impingement
- [[bursitis]]
