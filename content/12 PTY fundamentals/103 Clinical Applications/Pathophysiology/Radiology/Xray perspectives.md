### Special views

#### Skyline
for [[Patellofemoral Joint]] view, slightly flexed position, top down view
![[Pasted image 20250720105402.png|center]]

#### Y-scapula
oblique view such that scapula 'aligns' to humerus, forming distinct Y shape
+: view requires little motion for patients in a lot of pain
-: very restricted view of the shoulder joints, gives less information e.g. [[#Inferior superior]] view 
![[Pasted image 20250720111249.png|center|450]]
#### Apical Oblique

[Usefulness in acute trauma](https://ajronline.org/doi/pdf/10.2214/ajr.149.1.113)
TLDR, consider some views may be better for pointing out certain pathologies
Apical oblique good for [[Hill sachs lesion]] compared to [[#Shoulder in ER|AP view]]
