
## Overview
symptoms to look out for during imaging
- joint space narrowing → implies reduction in cartilage cover
- osteophytes → small bony projections around [[Reading Bone Xrays#Cortical margins|joint margins]], theorized to be response to reduce pressure on joint 
- scelorsis → increased density in bone at [[Reading Bone Xrays#Cortical margins|joint margins]]

## Kellgran-Lawrence grading
| Grade | Description                                                                                 |
| ----- | ------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| 1     | doubtful narrowing of joint space, possible osteophytic lipping                             |
| 2     | definite narrowing + ostephytes                                                             |
| 3     | definite narrowing + moderate multiple osteophytes,noticeable sclerosis, possible deformity |
| 4     | marked narrowing, large osteophytes, severe sclerosis, definite deformity                   |
