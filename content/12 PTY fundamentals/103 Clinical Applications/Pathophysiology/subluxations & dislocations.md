Dislocation = complete separation of joint surfaces 
subluxation = partial dislocation of articulating surfaces of joints, still partially in contact

## Stable Dislocation
- one time event, low recurrence
- Joint remains in place under normal function
- Minimal soft tissue damage

--- column-break ---

## Unstable Dislocation
- high recurrence 
- can redislocate with minimal trauma or movement
- involves soft tissue damage
