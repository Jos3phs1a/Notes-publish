## Prognosis
Depends on:
- Severity aka [[#Classifications]] 
- ligament injured
	e.g. AITFL [[03d Ligamentous Injuries#High ankle sprains|High ankle sprains]] must be treated early similar to fracture, if not physio intervention not as strong VS ATFL [[03d Ligamentous Injuries#Lateral|Lateral sprains]] deficits can still be tackled in chronic phase
- Stage of injury
	e.g. Chronic phase of [[04 PTY/111 MSK 1/Knee/Ligamentous injuries#Knee ligaments 6e01d6 ACL Injury|ACL injury]] won't expect to heal well vs treat in acute phase
- Personal factors: lifestyle, risk factors etc.

## Evolution of Treatment Principles
Previous practice: 
pure immobilization for 2 months then start rehab after
cons: atrophy, [[Contractures]]

New practice:
acute phase: immobilize
subacute phase: ↓ immobilize, begin mobilization to tolerance
exercise = orient fibers to strengthen ability in [[Healing Process#Inflammatory|Inflammatory phase]] where fibers become 'messy'
