## Presentation
- onset of pain after fixed time/distance
- increase in bulk at lower extremity during exercise
- lasts 10-20 min after cessation of activity

## Diagnosis 
- provocation tests - timed runs
- compartment pressure test

## management
- surgery → most times physio insufficient 
- running on softer surfaces, orthotics, cross training, optimize strength/flexibility of core and LL, gait training
