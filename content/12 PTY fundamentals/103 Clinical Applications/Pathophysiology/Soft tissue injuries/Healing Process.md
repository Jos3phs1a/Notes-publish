### [[Inflammation]] 
phagocytes clearing foreign particles entering through the wound
- day to weeks
- inflammatory cells invade injury area, remove debris
- release cytokines promote growth

### [[Proliferation]] 
Cells work to cover up wound, reforming skin layers 
- weeks to month
- fibroblasts, endothelial cells fill up gaps as **scar tissue**, mainly ==type 3 collagen== + capillaries


### Remodeling 
Body fine tunes and restore function at site to before injury e.g. nerve endings, glands + removal of scar etc.
involves parallel process of synthesis & degradation
- months to years
- ↓ cell density of endothelial cells, ↓ capillaries
- **Type 3 collagen** replaced by **type 1 collagen**, but ==healed portion will not be as "pure" as original== proportion → expect ↓ overall performance
- full recovery ==4-6 months==
