not the same as [[01b Continuum model of Tendinopathy|tendinopathy]] but can be related

involves the partial/complete discontinuation of [[Tendons|tendon]] fibers typically around 2-3cm above insertion point to bone

may be **retractive** ie distance between disrupted points may widen, implications on healing

## Presentation
- pain, localized tenderness
- loss of function 
more weak than pain due to damage of nociceptive afferents

### Physical exam
MMT: assess for weakness
Special test: weakness/loss of function
e.g. 
- drop arm sign for supraspinatus tendon tear
- thompson's & simmond's test for [[03e Ankle Tendinopathies#Achilles|Achilles injury]] 

--- column-break ---

### Imaging
ultrasound/MRI: assess extent of damage

--- end-multi-column

## Treatment
- Analgesia - pain control
- High grade ruptures require immobilisation in acute stage, possible surgical intervention
- subacute onwards: optimal biomechanical loading and rest of kinetic chain
