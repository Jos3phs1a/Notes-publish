# Ruptures

- 97% of spontaneous tendon ruptures have underlying pathology, highlight importance of [[01 Pathology and aetiology#Individual predispositions|Predisposing factors]] 
- 80% of individuals were asymptomatic before rupture → need for proactive management and surveillance

# Partial Tear

- [[History taking]] crucial
- symptoms include acute episode, loss of strength, pain

## Evaluation
### Size of tear
Determining size of tear is valuable information about severity and extent of injury
### Gap between torn fibers
helps assess degree of disruption within tendon and indicate severity
### Percentage of intact fibers
- helps classify tear based on extent of tendon damage
- guide treatment decision and prognosis
