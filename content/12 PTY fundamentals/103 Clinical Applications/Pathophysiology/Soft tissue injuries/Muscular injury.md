# Muscular strain/ruptures

## Presentation
- caused by excessive tensile +/- shear forces
- Pain on active contraction and passive stretch
- ↓ strength due to pain
- ↓ ROM

## Classification
note: grade does not correlate to healing time, progress through rehab has greater impact 

| Grade | Description                                            |
| ----- | ------------------------------------------------------ |
| 0     | clinical presentation w/o imaging                      |
| 1     | minor strain; pain with no/minimal loss of strength    |
| 2     | partial tear; greater pain, swelling, loss of strength |
| 3     | complete tear                                          |

## Treatment
- reduce symptoms, pain, swelling
- restore neuromuscular control at slow speeds
- prevent scar formation
- protect healing fibers
- gentle mobilisation and ROM
- gradual intensity, neuromuscular training, eccentric resistance training
