### Definition

Resistance palpated by examiner when moving joint passively to end of its range; resistance reflects joint structure and function.

| Normal End-feel | Description          | Example                     |
| --------------- | -------------------- | --------------------------- |
| Soft            | Soft tissue approx   | knee flexion                |
|                 | muscular,            | Hip flexion, knee straight  |
| Firm            | capsular(joints)     | Extension of MCP of fingers |
|                 | ligamentous          | Forearm supination          |
| Hard            | Bone contacting bone | Elbow extension             |

| End-feel | Description                                                                                                                               | Example                                                                        |
| -------- | ----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- | ------------------------------------------------------------------------------ |
| Soft     | Boggy feel ,occurs sooner or later in ROM than usual or in joint that usually has firm or hard end-feel                                   | soft tissue oedema, synovitis                                                  |
| Firm     | occurs sooner or later in ROM than usual or in joint that usually has firm or hard end-feel                                               | increased muscle tonus capsular, muscular, ligamentous and fascial shorterning |
| Hard     | Bony grating or bony block, occurs sooner or later in ROM than is usual or in a joint that normally has a soft or firm end-feel           | osteoarthritis, loose bodies in joint, fracture                                |
| Empty    | no resistance, patient’s protective muscle splinting or muscle spasm, lack of end-feel due to pain preventing patient reaching end of ROM | Bursitis, fracture, abscess                                                    |
