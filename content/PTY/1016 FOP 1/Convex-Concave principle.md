If bone with convex joint surface moves on the bone with concave surface, convex joint surface slides in the direction opposite to the bone segment’s rolling motion.
Involves both principles of [[Movement Terms#Osteokinematics|Osteokinematics]] & [[Movement Terms#Arthrokinematics|Arthrokinematics]]

TLDR:

Convex-on-concave:

convex rolls and slides opposite directions

Concave-on-convex surface movement:

concave rolls and slides same directions
