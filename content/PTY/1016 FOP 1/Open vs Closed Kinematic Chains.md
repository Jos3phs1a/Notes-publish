**Kinematic Chain:**

concept to describe interconnectedness of movement segments and muscles to describe human movements.
**Open Kinematic Chain(OKC)**

when distal segment of chain “moves freely w/o any external resistance

e.g. chest press

**Closed Kinematic Chain(CKC)**

when distal segment “meets a ‘considerable external resistance’ that restrains free motion”

e.g. push-ups

Difference: Squat vs Leg extensions
Target muscles: Quads
Squat → feet locked, not able to move freely → Closed
Leg Ext → feet can move freely ie rotate, plantar/dorsiflex → Open
