### Types of Muscle Force Generation

Mostly involves Active contraction 
- Isometric
    - force generation, w/o lengthening muscles
    - inter torque = external torque
- Concentric(positive work)
    - Force generated through muscle shortening
    - Internal toque>external torque (muscle exerts more force to lift weight through motion)
- Eccentric(negative work) braking,slowing down movement
    - Force generated through muscle lengthening
    - Internal torque> external torque (load exerted on muscle causes it to lengthen)
