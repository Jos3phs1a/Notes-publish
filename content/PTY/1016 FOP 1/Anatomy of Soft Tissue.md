
### Skeletal Muscles
Function: contract & generate force for movement
Composition: fibers & myofibrils

Note: no collagen/elastin
	Contractions: concentric, eccentric, isometric
	[[Mechanics of Soft Tissue#^e88993|Viscoelastic]] material behaviour, have internal resistance to motion
### Tendons & Ligaments


| Tendons                                 | Ligaments                                   |
| --------------------------------------- | ------------------------------------------- |
| Enable muscle to transfer force to bone | Stability, Prevent bones from excess motion |
| High strength & stiffness               | Low strength & stiffness                    |
| Low extensibility                       | High extensibility                          |
