**Types of Joints**

Various joint structure types:

- synarthrodial
    - fused together via synarthrodial sutures e.g. skull
    - immovable
- amphiarthrodial
    - slightly moveable e.g. invertebrae joints, 1st intercostal joint

diarthrodial (synovial)

uniaxial ,biaxial,(one rotates over another) triaxial movements

- hinge
- condyloid /ellipsoidal
- saddle
- pivot
- ball and socket.

 [[Convex-Concave principle]]


### **Closed-packed joints**

surface of joint segments, match each other perfectly, in ONLY ONE position of congruency

- max surface area contact
- ligaments are farthest apart and under tension,
- capsular structures are taut
- joint mechanically compressed, hard to distract(separate)

### **Loose-packed joints**

joint surfaces do not match perfectly, incongruent

- Ligamentous and capsular structures are slack
- Joint surfaces may be distracted several millimeters
- Allow necessary motions of spin, roll, slide

Joint’s resting pos- there is least congruency at which capsule and ligaments are loosest, most slack
