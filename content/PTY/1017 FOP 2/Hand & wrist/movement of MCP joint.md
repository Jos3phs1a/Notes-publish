## Flexion
Frontal plane/anterior-posterior axis of rotation

--- start-multi-column: ID_xuzh
```column-settings
Number of Columns: 2
Largest Column: standard
```

Flexion: 
1) FDP activates, tendons course through [[Structure of MCP joints#Pulleys|A1 & A2 pulleys]] 
2) ==concave surface of phalanx== slides + rolls palmar direction on fixed ==convex metacarpal head== 
3) [[Structure of MCP joints#Dorsal Capsule|Dorsal Capsule]] & [[Structure of MCP joints#Collateral ligaments|Radial collateral ligaments]] become taut

2) [[Structure of MCP joints#Palmar plates|Palmar Plate]] becomes taut, Cord portion of [[Structure of MCP joints#Collateral ligaments|Radial collateral ligament]] slacken

## Abduction/Adduction
sagittal plane/media-lateral axis of rotation

--- start-multi-column: ID_2052
```column-settings
Number of Columns: 2
Largest Column: standard
```

Abduction:
- ==Concave surface proximal phalanx== rolls and slides outward(radial or ulnar may change depending on which digit)  on ==convex surface metacarpal head==
- [[Structure of MCP joints#Collateral ligaments|ulnar collateral ligament]] becomes taut [[Structure of MCP joints#Collateral ligaments|radial collateral ligament]] slacken

Reverse is applied for Adduction
