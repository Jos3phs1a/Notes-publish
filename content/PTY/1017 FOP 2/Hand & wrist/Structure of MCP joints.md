Consist 3 structures

## Collateral ligaments
 2 sections: 
 - **radial collateral** 
 - **ulnar collateral**
	 proximal attachment: on posterior tubercle of metacarpal head 

Midway, split into 2 parts:
**Cord:** 
- more dorsal portion, thicker stronger
- distal attachment: base of proximal phalange
**Accessory:**
 - thinner, fan-shaped
 - distal attachment: along edge of [[Structure of MCP joints#Palmar plates|palmar plate]] 
 
## Palmar plates
Thick dense fibrocartilage on palmar aspect of joint
function: 
- strengthen the structure of MCP joints, 
- limit the extremes of extension

## Dorsal Capsule
Ligamentous structure 
- part of joint capsule, 
Function: 
 - stability and protection
## Pulleys
Not part of the MCP joint!!!
Type of ligament not 
Functions:
- House the FDP tendons for efficient finger flexion
- Prevent bowstringing 
- Provides fulcrum to elicit movement
