When assessing hip pathologies
- Must differentiate between local or referred pain ie,
	important to first rule out [[lumbar pathologies]] & SIJ issues these regions can also generate hip pain
- Rule out ==local== [[02b Anterior EA hip pathologies]] vs ==vague== pain [[02a IA pathologies of hip]] 
## tell tale signs
### neurological features 
most common signs pins and needles, stabby/sharp/throbbing pain numbing or pains that radiate down the leg

### Mechanical 
patient in Lumbar spine during hip movement


After ruled out, can begin to rule in [[02 Hip pathologies]]
