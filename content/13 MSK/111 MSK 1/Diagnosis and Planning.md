## Coming up with differentials
Using Information from [[History taking]] and knowledge of pathologies, infer possible conditions that could be attributed to patient's problems

List Supporting & Negating for each possible condition 
## Must Should Could
Goal of MSC: structured format of ruling in conditions 

Must: Will definitely rule in condition, must perform

Should: IF HAVE TIME/ PATIENT CAN TOLERATE get more in depth info about ruled in condition rule out other conditions

Could: Last resort, only when must and should all negative
