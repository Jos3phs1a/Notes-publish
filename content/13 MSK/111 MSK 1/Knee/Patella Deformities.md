# Patella alta
- high position of patella due to elongation of infrapatellar tendon, 
- is a risk factor for patella [[subluxations & dislocations|subluxation]]
- NOT THE SAME AS [[#Patella tendon rupture]]

may lead to:
- instability
- pain
- [[Patellofemoral Joint]] dysfunction
