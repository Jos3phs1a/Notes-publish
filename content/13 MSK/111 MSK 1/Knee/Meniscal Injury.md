### Mechanism of injury
#### Acute/Traumatic
- athletic population
- Sudden twisting/ rapid change of direction
- shear stress generated in compression combine with rotation
#### Degenerative
- Age related
- 60% of individuals older than 65 yo
- Repetitive activities

--- column-break ---

### Symptoms
- delayed effusion
- catching or locking sensation
- pain with hyperextension or maximal passive flexion
- difficulty squatting and kneeling
- tender on joint line palpation
