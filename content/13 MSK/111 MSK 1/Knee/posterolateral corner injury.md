### Mechanism of Injury
- Direct force to anteromedial tibia
- excess ==varus force== to flexed knee
- Knee ==hyperextension== during contact or non contact injury
- excess tibial external rotation with partially flexed knee

--- column-break ---

### Symptoms
- Lateral/posterolateral rotatory instability
- 'giving way' sensation in the knee
- varus thrust or posterolateral hyperextension thrust gait
- knee 'thrusts' outwards or backwards and outwards respectively during weight bearing phase
- peroneal nerve involvement

--- end-multi-column

### Special Test
- varus stress 
- prone dial 
- External rotation recurvatum
- posterolateral draw
- reverse pivot shift
