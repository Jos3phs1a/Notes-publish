## Anterior impingement
**Impinged structure:** affects anterior talus, tibia, fibula
**Causes:** repetitive actions → abnormal calcification within joint 
**Presentation:** chronic pain on dorsiflexion


--- column-break ---

## Posterior impingement
**Impinged structures:** Stieda processes, Os Trigonum, prominent posterior calcaneal tuberosities, downward sloping tibia.
**Causes:** compression of talus + surrounding soft tissue between tibia, calcaneus during ==plantarflexion==
**Presentation:** chronic inflammation and pain on push-off, ==plantarflexion(A/P)==

## Anterior
**Impinged structure:** flexor hallucis longus tendon, [[Ankle ligaments#Deltoid ligament|Deltoid ligaments]], joint capsule
**Causes:** distal fascicle of anterior inferior tibiofibular ligament (DFAITFL) scar tissue formation/meniscoid lesions/impingement. Anterior soft tissue impingement usually result of recurrent [[03d Ligamentous Injuries#Lateral|ankle sprain]]
**Presentation:** pain and edema during extreme ==dorsiflexion or eversion==, repetitive activities


--- column-break ---

## Posterior
**Impinged structure:** same as [[#Anterior]] 
**Causes:** injury to anterior [[Ankle ligaments|Medial & lateral ligaments]], leading to hypertrophy/forming accessory masses
**Presentation:** Pain and disability during ==plantarflexion & eversion==
