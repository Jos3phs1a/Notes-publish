# Figure of eight measurement

reliable and valid in measuring [[edema]] due to [[03d Ligamentous Injuries|sprains]] or other disorders

**Patient position**: long sitting both feet over end of plinth, ankle in approx. 20° plantarflexion
**Measurement**: use plastic tape
1) pull tape midway between tendon of tib ant and lateral mal
2) pull tape medially to instep, distal to tuberosity of the navicular 
3) draw tape across arch of foot just proximal to base of 5th Metatarsal
4) continue across tib ant tendon, around ankle joint just distal to medial mal, across achilles tendon
5) circle tape around ankle, ending distal to lateral mal
