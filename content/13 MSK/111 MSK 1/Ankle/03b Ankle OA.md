## Overview

**Structures:**
- [[Distal tibiofibular Joint]], articular cartilage, subchondral bone, synovial membrane, ligaments
- tibia: distal end
- talus: superior aspect
- Ligaments: [[Ankle ligaments#Lateral Collateral ligaments|LCLs]] + [[Ankle ligaments#Deltoid ligament|MCLs]]

**MOI:** 
- **Primary OA**: Rare idiopathic
- **Secondary OA**: Predominantly post traumatic, 75% - 80% of cases due to [[03a Fractures|Fractures]] or Chronic [[03d Ligamentous Injuries|ligament injuries]]  


--- column-break ---

## Clinical Presentation
- pain and tenderness at joint line
- swelling & stiffness, especially after activity or prolonged periods of rest
- ↓ ROM & difficulty walking → altered gait 
- crepitus
## Risk factors
- Trauma: history of [[03a Fractures|fractures]]
- [[Chronic ankle instability]]: recurrent ankle [[03d Ligamentous Injuries|sprains]] leading to ligamentous laxity
- Age: older more susceptible
- Overuse: high impact activities strain joint
- Obesity
