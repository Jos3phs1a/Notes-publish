aka tennis elbow

overuse injury of forearm extensors resulting in tendinopathy

### symptoms
- inflammation/[[edema]] - radial collateral ligament, anconeus muscle , radial bursa, 
- thickening or partial tears in common extensor origin
- tendon calcfication
- full ROM
- decrease grip strength
- pain at lateral epicondyle during manual work

### Physical Exam
- stress extensor muscles
- stability of elbow
