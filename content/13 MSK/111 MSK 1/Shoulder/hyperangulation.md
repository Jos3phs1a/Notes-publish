During overhead throwing actions: humeral shaft goes beyond plane of the body of scapula during the cocking position of throwing. 

**normal circumstances:**  scapula undergoes ==retraction + horizontal abduction== of humerus. 

**abnormal circumstances:** humeral shaft ==fail to remain in same plane of movement== encroachment of rotator cuff tendons between the humeral head and the glenoid rim cause internal impingement symptoms.
