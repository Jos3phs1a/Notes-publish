Overuse beyond tolerable load causes degeneration and changes in [[01 Pathology and aetiology|collagen matrix]]  

## Presentation
- hard to deduce due to complexity of shoulder and similarities with other pathologies, best to use ruling out approach

### possible signs
- pain in overhead activity
- motion less than 90° of abduction usually painfree. 
- painful arc on abduction between approximately 70° - 120°.
- may have tenderness over ==supraspinatus tendon== proximal to or at ==GT of humerus.== If the ==infraspinatus== is involved, tenderness on insertion ==dorsally== on GT.

- **Strength**: Generally **preserved or only mildly reduced** due to pain inhibition, not structural failure.
- **Range of Motion (ROM)**: Near-normal active ROM, but may be painful esp in end range.
different from  [[01c rotator cuff tears]]  

### Physical exam
- ROM to see pain during range
- MMT for insufficiency 
- painful arc, Neer's, hawkins kennedy


## treament
divided into 2 parts
1) treat symptoms
2) rehab of rotator cuff
	- restore joint arthrokinematics
	- muscular strength balance and local muscle strength
