# Overview

aka GH internal rotation deficit(GIRD)

- result of both capsular tightness and muscular contracture.
- associated with [[03 Glenohumeral joint instability#Acquired sport specific instability(AIOS)|AIOS]] 

**MOI:** load on posterior shoulder during deceleration phase of throwing motion → microtrauma

**symptoms:** decreased adduction and internal rotation mobility

## assessment
- ROM
# Treatment

- treat tightness through stretching/manual therapy
	- accessory glides
	- sleeper stretch, passive IR on sidelying, arm flexed at 90° ![[Pasted image 20250713162150.png|center|450]]
	- cross body stretch, elevate arm to 90°, passive horizontal adduction
