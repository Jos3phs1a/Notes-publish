# SLAP lesions
**Location:** Superior, Anterior, Posterior region of GL suffered lesions/damage e.g. partial/full tear 
**MOI:** Repetitive throwing, hyperextension, heavy lifting, direct trauma

### Presentation
- pain, clicking, popping with shoulder movement
- ↓ glenohumeral IR ROM
- pain with overhead motions
- ↓ rotator cuff strength and endurance
- ↓ scapular stabiliser strength and endurance
- inability to lie on the affected shoulder
### classification
| Type | Description                                                                                             | Biceps involved/Stable/unstable | Treatment                 |
|:----:| ------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |:-------------------------------:| ------------------------- |
|  I   | glenoid and labrum attached<br>fraying & degeneration                                                   |           No, stable            | rehab                     |
|  II  | Detachment of superior labrum + LHB tendon from glenoid rim                                             |          Yes,unstable           | surgery followed by rehab |
| III  | Bucket handle tear<br>meniscoid superior labrum torn away, displaced <br>LHB tendon labral rim attached |            No,stable            | ^                         |
| IIII | Bucket-handle tear of labrum extending into biceps tendon                                               |          Yes, unstable          | ^                         |
### Physical Exam
- Bicep load tests 1 & 2
